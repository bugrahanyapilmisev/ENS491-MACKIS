Emeritus Faculty Member Instructions

IHR-S470-07-V06
Effective Date
29-12-2008
Update Date
29-08-2024
Related Unit / Owner
HR / İnsan Kaynakları Direktörlüğü
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Aim
To determine the policy and rules regarding the Emeritus Faculty Membership as an honorary appointment.
Scope
This instruction letter applies to the full-time permanent faculty members and academic administrators having at least doctorate degree. Academic administrators who do not have a doctorate degree are not included in this scope. Instructors and temporary faculty members are not in the scope of this instruction letter. The first appointments to the “Emeritus Faculty Membership” took place on June 30, 2010, as well as the retirements due to age limit.
Principle
Sabancı University believes in the necessity of recruiting the young and best minds as well as benefiting from the abilities, knowledge and experience of the experienced faculty members. At the same time, Sabancı University gives importance to the scholarly activities of the retired faculty members and wants to support their productivity as well as their continuous involvement in the University activities.
Statutory Basis
Definitions / Abbreviations

University: Sabancı University

Faculty Member: Faculty members and academic administrators having at least doctorate degree

HR: Human Resources

Deans Council: The Council consisting of the Faculty Deans and the President

Executive Board: It consists of four members: The Chair of the Board of Trustees, the Vice Chair of the Board of Trustees, the President, and the Secretary General. It carries out the duties assigned to it by the Board of Trustees.

Board of Trustees: Sabancı University Board of Trustees, (The highest decision-making body of the University and representing its legal entity)

Emeritus Faculty Member: It is a status granted to our professors who have reached retirement age but whose knowledge, experience, international reputation and relationships will be an asset for many years to come in guiding both academics and students.

Academic Administrator: President, Vice President, Dean, Academic Director (Individuals who perform academic and administrative management roles together)

Regulations

1. CRITERIA FOR THE “EMERITUS FACULTY MEMBERSHIP”: 

  • Being a permanent full-time Faculty Member of the University for at least 15 years
  • Having an outstanding academic career differentiating them from the peers
  • Giving a distinguished service to the academic unit, the University, society and the scholarly community at large
  • Being at least 65 and at most 68 years old and being eligible for Sabancı University Retirement or SGK Retirement.

Emeritus Faculty Membership appointments outside the scope of the directive are an exception and these honorary appointments are decided by the Board of Trustees.

2. APPOINTMENT PROCESS:

  • At the beginning of each academic semester, HR prepares a list of the faculty members who will reach the age of 65 and 15 years of service within the third semester from then on and sends the list to the Faculty Deans, while the list of academic administrators is sent to the President.
  • For faculty members, the Deans of the relevant faculties and the President, and for academic administrators, the President recommends “Emeritus” Faculty Member candidates from this list. (For faculty members, it is recommended that they be recommended by the Dean and the President.)
  • The candidates are discussed in the first Deans Council.  
  • After the President’s approval, the selected candidate(s) are presented to the Executive Board and the Board of Trustees.  The candidate(s) approved by the Executive Board and the Board of Trustees are appointed as “Emeritus Faculty Member of Sabancı University”.

At the end of each academic term, the people who will be appointed as "Emeritus" Faculty Members after two academic terms are announced and the Emeritus Award ceremony is held.

 

3. PRIVILEGES AND BENEFITS OF EMERITUS FACULTY MEMBERS

The names of “SU Emeritus Faculty Members” take place in the University roster.

All “Emeritus Faculty Members” are entitled to the privileges and benefits stated below whether they undertake any job or not:

Sabancı University may revoke or change the scope of these privileges and benefits with prior notice.

  3.a. Monetary Benefits

SU Emeritus Faculty Members will benefit from the following benefits following their retirement from the University upon reaching the age of at least 65:

•Severance payment, irrespective of retirement from the social security institution.

• The individual is entitled to receive the savings within the scope of the Sabancı University Private Pension Plan. The individual's savings within the scope of this plan vary depending on the period of premium payment made under the Group Private Retirement Plan and the salaries received during this period. Information regarding the scope of the Private Pension Plan is specified in the Employees Group Private Retirement Plan Instruction Letter (IHR-S470-03)

  3.b. Other Benefits

SU Emeritus Faculty Members are entitled to the benefits stated below. Sabancı University may revoke or change the scope of these privileges and benefits with prior notice.

  • University Identification Card

They will receive the “SU Emeritus Faculty Member ID Card”, with which they can enter the University. Also a car sticker would be issued for them.

  • Private Health Insurance

“Emeritus Faculty Members” will be entitled to continue their Private Health Insurance, covering treatment expenses for illnesses or accidents. The premiums of this insurance will be paid by the University.Sabancı University may revoke or change the insurance coverage with prior notice.

Also the Emeritus Faculty Members’ spouses and children (provided that they are students) up to the age of 24, would be included in the Health Insurance Plan, if premiums are paid by the faculty member. The yearly premiums are negotiated with the insurance company and announced every year. Emeritus Faculty Members and their dependents, who would like to benefit from the Health Insurance, have to continue to the plan with no interruption. According to the conditions of the insurance plan, in case of any interruption, the individual cannot be insured again. 

  • Information Technology Services and Facilities

Emeritus Faculty Members can continue to use the SU e-mail address by updating it to name.surname@emeritus.sabanciuniv.edu. However, in the event of unfair competition or conflict of interest, the University has the right to close the e-mail address at any time. In addition, Emeritus Faculty Members who actively teach can benefit from the rights granted to University employees in the computer software license agreements made by the University, under the same terms and conditions. If they leave the institution completely and stop teaching, the use of these opportunities also ends.

  • Information Center Services and Facilities

SU Emeritus Faculty Members may benefit from the services and facilities of the Information Center with their SU Identification Card. For more detailed information, please see the Instruction Letter for Benefiting from Information Center (IIC-840-02).

  • Sports Center Services and Facilities

SU Emeritus Faculty Members, their spouses and their children may benefit from the Sports Center with their SU Identification Card. For more detailed information, please see the Instruction Letter for Management of Sports Facilities and Supplies (ISR-C630-03).

  • Participation in Activities Organized for Staff

SU Emeritus Faculty Members are invited and welcomed to join all kinds of activities organized for staff, such as opening and commencement ceremonies, concerts, etc. in the same conditions as staff members.

  • Tuition Fee Discount for Children

The children of the Emeritus Faculty Members can benefit from the tuition fee discount in the same conditions as the children of SU staff. For more detailed information please see the Instruction Letter for Determination of Tuition Fees and Follow-up of Payments, ISR-C170-01:

  • Club Memberships

SU Emeritus Faculty Members may be honorary members of the Student’s Club, as SU staff.

 

4. EMPLOYMENT TYPES OF SABANCI UNIVERSITY EMERITUS FACULTY MEMBERS:

Individuals appointed as Emeritus Faculty Members may undertake the following temporary duties and responsibilities, in line with the needs of the University, after approval by the Board of Trustees:

• Emeritus Faculty Membership Adjunct / Network Faculty Membership

• Annual and Semester-based Part-time Faculty Membership

•Research

•Consultancy

The job that the Emeritus Faculty Member will undertake and the suggested employment type together with the contract terms and conditions will be offered by the President to the Executive Board for approval.

In case they undertake any job in the University, they are not expected to hold executive positions in the University or vote in the faculty councils.

Depending on the type of job and the program/ research of the faculty member, additional benefits stated below may be provided to Emeritus Faculty Members. These benefits are aimed to fulfill the academic and scientific needs, which are in line with the University’s mission and are limited with the resources of the University:

•Only Full-Time Emeritus Faculty Members are included in the Personal Research Fund for the first three (3) years. Part-Time Emeritus Faculty Members are not included in this scope.

•Office or laboratory use may be used according to faculty availability. Part-Time Emeritus Faculty Members are not included in this scope.

If a decision is made to work full/part time with Emeritus Faculty Members, a salary is paid according to the terms of the contract to be signed, and no payment is made to them if they do not assume a duty at the University. Additional opportunities and benefits that may be provided according to the type of responsibility the person will carry out during their duty at the University are discussed in the Deans' Council and presented to the Executive Board with the approval of the President. After the approval of the Executive Board and the Board of Trustees, the Emeritus Faculty Member is appointed.

5. CONTRACT TYPES WITH SABANCI UNIVERSITY EMERITUS FACULTY MEMBERS:

The following contract alternatives are applied to individuals appointed as Emeritus Faculty Members, depending on their working type:  

  1. Full-Time Emeritus Faculty Membership:

Full-time contracts of Emeritus Faculty Members are made on an academic year basis (Fall+Spring semesters) following the date they receive the title of “Emeritus Faculty Member” and are renewed for up to three (3) years. The remuneration due to the contract change is arranged as half of the current faculty member remuneration. The remuneration increase during the contract renewal period is made within the scope of the university's remuneration policy.

  1. Part-Time Emeritus Faculty Membership: Contracts of Part-Time Emeritus Faculty Members are made in two (2) types. After the first three academic years (Fall + Spring semesters) of Emeritus Faculty Membership are completed, they can teach courses for one academic year (Fall + Spring semesters) or on a semester basis.

2.a Part-Time Emeritus Faculty Membership on an academic year basis; Contracts of Emeritus Faculty Members who will work within this scope are made for a fixed term of one (1) year. The remuneration is based on the number of courses given during the academic term. Between July 1 and August 31, the remuneration payment continues despite the fact that no active course load is scheduled. At the end of the contract period, the contract can be renewed upon faculty request.

2.b Part-Time Emeritus Faculty Membership on an academic term basis: Contracts of Emeritus Faculty Members who will work within this scope are made for a fixed term of one (1) term. The remuneration is based on the number of courses given during the academic term. A new contract is made for each term.

**No payment is made to them if they do not undertake a duty at the university.
