Membership Instruction Letter (IIC-C840-01)

Instruction No
IIC-C840-01
Effective Date
21-01-2002
Update Date
12-09-2025
Related Unit / Owner
IC / Bilgi Merkezi Direktörlüğü
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Aim
To determine membership rules to ensure that Sabancı University members and external users can benefit from the Information Center, and to support them in identifying and meeting their information needs in accordance with these rules.
Scope
This includes the processes for membership and use of the services offered by the Information Center by Sabancı University members and external users.
Principle
The Information Center, established to meet the information and documentation needs of Sabancı University members and external users in the most effective way, allows researchers who need it to benefit from the knowledge within the Center, in accordance with specific rules.
Statutory Basis

Law No. 5846 on Intellectual and Artistic Works and relevant legislation.

Definitions / Abbreviations

Call Tracking System: This system is used to carry out work related to the processes at the Information Center, particularly the execution, monitoring, supervision, and completion of work.

Copyright: The "exclusive right" granted by the rights holder to any original work in the literary, scientific, or artistic fields, granting others permission to use their work. The Information Center manages the copying or use of information resources in its collections in accordance with Law No. 5846 on Intellectual and Artistic Works and related legislation.

Docserver: This is a shared storage space provided by the Information Technology Unit for the use of other units, accessible by authorized employees, and used over the network.

Document Procurement: This is the procurement of articles not currently in the Information Center's collections from other institutions.

External Users: Trade and industrial companies, non-governmental organizations and public enterprises, other university members, and those not mentioned above.

ILL (Interlibrary Loan): This service involves borrowing books not held by the Information Center from other institutions in accordance with their terms of use, returning them at the end of their term, and fulfilling similar requests from other institutions.

LIAW: The Law No. 5846 on Intellectual and Artistic Works.

Protocol: Agreements made with institutions and organizations for the purpose of collaboration and publication.

Sabancı University Members: Sabancı University's academic and teaching "Emeritus" staff, administrative staff, undergraduate, graduate, and exchange students, alumni, retirees, and non-teaching Emeritus retirees.

SU-Net Username and Password: This is the username and password provided by the Information Technologies Unit, which provides all Sabancı University members with access to their SU-Net Accounts.

SUFORM: This is a workflow application program.

SÜMED: Sabancı University Alumni Association.

Regulations

A. SABANCI UNIVERSITY MEMBERS

Sabancı University Academic, Emeritus, and Administrative Staff, Undergraduate, Graduate, and Exchange Students become natural members of the Information Center with their University-issued IDs and can use all the services and facilities offered by the Information Center in accordance with the rules.

B. EXTERNAL USERS

External users wishing to use the Information Center's services and facilities can request access by sending an email to reference@sabanciuniv.edu. Those deemed eligible are granted a daily access permit and can use the services and facilities on-site.

External users wishing to use the Information Center's services and facilities for a longer period must apply by completing the "Information Center Membership and Benefit Request Form (FIC-C84001-01)". Users who are approved for membership can use the services and facilities after paying the membership fee, in accordance with their chosen membership.

1. TRADE AND INDUSTRIAL COMPANIES

PACKAGE 1

  • Trade and industrial companies must apply to benefit from the Information Center services and facilities by completing the "Information Center Membership and Benefit Request Form (FIC-C84001-01)" and selecting "Package 1" on the form. Users who are deemed eligible for membership can benefit from the services and facilities for one year after paying the membership fee, depending on their chosen membership.
  • Those who are deemed ineligible will be notified by email.
  • A photocopy of their ID card and one passport-sized photograph will be required for those deemed eligible.
  • Institutions that complete the process will receive a maximum of 3 one-year ID cards that can be used simultaneously.
  • Institutional users with ID cards can benefit from the services and facilities on-site.
  • External users can benefit from the Information Center services and facilities on-site until 11:00 PM daily.
  • Institutions that are members of “Package 1” can benefit from the Reference / Information service and Databases (in line with their license agreements) on-site.
  • Institutions cannot use electronic information resources remotely, in accordance with license agreements signed by the Sabancı University Information Center and the "LIAW” and relevant legislation.
  • Institutions may change the users who use the Information Center on their behalf twice a year.
  • Institutions may re-apply for a membership extension or package change.
  • The institution that has been granted membership rights is responsible for any problems that arise during use.
  • Institutions whose users do not comply with the usage rules will have their usage permission revoked, and membership fees will not be refunded. Subsequent applications will not be accepted for 3 years.
  • Those who can benefit from membership services and annual membership fees are updated and implemented upon submission to the General Secretary for approval when necessary.
  • If the user leaves the institution, the institution must notify the Information Center in writing within 3 days.

C. MEMBERSHIP PROCEDURE RULES

  1. Membership applications for external users are made through the website by completing the "Information Center Membership and Benefit Request Form (FIC-C84001-01)”. For requests submitted other than the request forms, the "Information Center Membership and Benefit Request Form (FIC-C84001-01)" must be completed.
  2. User record creation, user record update and cancellation information for academics, those who give lecture and work academically as titled "Emeritus" and administrative staff receives and confimed via e-mail from Human Resources Unit. 
  3. At the beginning of the academic year, new student information is collected from the Student Resources Unit, and this information is transferred to the Information Center system to create new student user records.
  4. During circulation activities or as needed, student information is reviewed in the mySU directory. When student information is checked and a difference in information is detected, an update is made to the user record.
  5. Information on academic and administrative staff whose university affiliation is terminated is reviewed. Any items requiring return, overdue fines, or lost or damage are entered into SUFORM, and the user's record is deactivated. For any unreturned items, the user and the relevant Faculty, Administrative Unit and Human Resources Unit are notified with information regarding the return of the resource, including the overdue fine and the status of the lost/damaged resource. In cases of delays, damage or lost, action is taken in accordance with the Circulation and Utilization Instruction Letter, and Human Resources Unit are notified. After all these procedures are completed, user records are deactivated for borrowing.
  6. A "Withdrawal Form" submitted by the student is collected for undergraduate, graduate, and exchange students who have been dismissed from the university for various reasons or who have been notified by email that they are on leave. Alumni must check their fines to the Information Center or the status of their account on the "Graduation" page. If students have items that need to be returned, they must return items; if they have overdue or other fines, they must pay their fines. All procedures required for graduation are suspended until these procedures are completed.
  7. Membership, renewal and cancellation procedures for alumni and retirees, trade and industrial companies, and members of other universities are communicated by the Reference and Marketing of Information Services Department.
  8. Sabancı University members who do not pay the document delivery fee, do not return, damage or lost items provided through interlibrary loan will have their Information Center membership registration blocked and their use of the circulation services suspended.
  9. Use of services is subject to the "LIAW and related legislation”. These procedures are carried out in accordance with the provisions of the "Corrective Action Form" (FGS-S620-03-01).
  10. Membership procedures are carried out as described above, based on user types, and procedures are conducted in accordance with these rules.
  11. Correspondence with members is conducted via a generic email address and is stored in the Information Center archives in accordance with the provisions of the Personal Data Protection Law.
  12. Activities and statistics are reported to the Management on a monthly / annual basis.
  13. Improvements / updates and requests received from users and / or detected during work carried out are reported to the Management by adding them to the "Corrective Action Form (FGS-S620-03-01)".
