Social Media Management Instruction Letter (IMIC-S530-02)

Instruction No
IMIC-S530-02
Effective Date
24-06-2024
Update Date
24-06-2024
Related Unit / Owner
MIC / Pazarlama ve Kurumsal İletişim Direktörlüğü
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Aim
This document was developed to determine the rules, conditions, and implementation steps for the announcement of social media activities of Sabancı University and all its affiliated bodies on the university's institutional social media accounts.
Scope
This document covers activities to ensure that topics such as conferences, seminars, panels, competitions, award ceremonies, reports, collaborations, and artistic activities produced by Sabancı University's faculties, centers, forums, all academic and administrative units, and alumni and student organizations are featured on relevant social media channels, in line with the publication plan established by the annual strategic communication plan and with appropriate visual design and content.
Principle
The goal is for all social media-worthy activities produced by the University to be featured and engage with on relevant channels.
Statutory Basis
Definitions / Abbreviations

MIC: Marketing and Institutional Communications Unit
Social Media: Sabancı University Institutional Social Media Accounts

Regulations

Rules
1. Sabancı University's institutional social media activities are conducted by the Marketing and Institutional Communications Unit. Relevant posts are planned on the university's institutional accounts, adhering to the social media strategy, which is prepared annually and updated monthly. For pages other than the university's institutional accounts, visuals and text are prepared by MIC upon request. (Pages on institutional accounts are the responsibility of MIC.)
2. Sabancı University's social media activities are planned within the framework of the annual work calendar, in collaboration with MIC and the units requesting the work, and following meetings with these units.
3. For unplanned projects not included in the annual plan and arising from immediate developments and needs, preliminary information about the project must be submitted to MIC via the MIC Request Form and email at least two weeks before the requested work date (processes may vary depending on the scope of the work and may vary after mutual consultation). MIC finalizes the social media plan required for the project by making new arrangements and revising the social media plan.
4. All visual and promotional materials used in communication activities must comply with the rules outlined in the Sabancı University Social Media Usage Guide.
5. The MIC Unit is responsible for ensuring that the content, visuals, and design of communication activities conform to the institutional identity at every stage of the process. The relevant unit is responsible for verifying the accuracy and appropriateness of all content. Projects are processed after the approval of the relevant unit and final review by MIC.
