Information Technology Affairs Management Instruction Letter (IIT-S130-01)

Instruction No
IIT-S130-01V14
Effective Date
13-01-2012
Update Date
13-03-2025
Related Unit / Owner
IT / Bilgi Teknolojileri Direktörlüğü
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Aim
To describe the design, commissioning, maintenance, and update activities of IT services.
Scope
All IT services in the service catalog are within the scope of this document.
Principle
In the operation of services provided by the IT unit, alignment with University strategies, parallel processes, and outputs are observed.
Statutory Basis
Definitions / Abbreviations

IT: Information Technologies
SGM: Sabancı Performing Arts Center
Service Catalog: Catalog containing IT services
Service Manager: IT employee responsible for the service

Regulations

1. User accounts and access to IT services are provided in accordance with the User Accounts and Access Management Procedure (PIT-S130-0115).

2. All stakeholders are provided with IT resources for their use within the university. In this context, employees are provided with standard hardware and software in accordance with the Hardware and Software Provision Procedure

(PIT-S130-0123).

3. Audio and visual technical support for classrooms and lecture halls, as well as for the cinema and SGM where events are held, is provided in accordance with the Hardware and Software Provision Procedure (PIT-S130-0123).

4. Access to the application software included in the service catalog is provided to users who need to use it for their duties. Access is provided in accordance with the Service Request Management Procedure (PIT-S130-0103).

5. All services provided by the IT Unit are accessed through the Service Catalog. IT managers update the service catalog.

6. Service interruptions are recorded in accordance with the Management of Service Interruptions (PIT-S130-0109). IT personnel responsible for the service are responsible for updating these records.

7. User help and training documents related to IT services are available on the IT website. Service personnel update the relevant IT service pages.

8. The management of the system room where IT services are provided is carried out in accordance with the System Room Management (PIT-S130-0128), and the management of the server systems to be installed in the system room is carried out in accordance with the Server Systems Management (PIT-S130-0112).

9. The IT Unit&#39;s annual budget plan is prepared in accordance with the Procedure for Managing Budget Preparations

(PIT-S130-0125).

10. The classification of corporate data processed by various applications and software is carried out in accordance with the Procedure for Institutional Data Management (PIT-S130-0129).
