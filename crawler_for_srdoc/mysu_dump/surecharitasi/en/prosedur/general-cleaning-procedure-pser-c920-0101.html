Related Instruction
Cleaning Services (ISER-C920-01)
Aim
The aim of this document is to lay out how the cleaning of indoor and outdoor areas of the University is done and followed up under the responsibility of the Cleaning Services Unit.
Scope
This document covers all cleaning-related activities of the Cleaning Services unit and cleaning company.
Definitions / Abbreviations

University: Sabancı University

Cleaning Services: Services Directorate / Cleaning Services Unit.

Cleaning company: The company from which the university receives services for the fulfillment of cleaning works.

Call Center: It is the unit at Sabancı University that collects requests by telephone and electronic form and opens work orders electronically to the relevant units.

SUTicket: Electronic recording software of requests collected by the Call Center.

Cleaning Checklist: It is the electronic checklist in which the cleaning company officer evaluates the interior of the building in terms of cleaning standards and technical faults following the cleaning service.

Cleaning Inspection Form: It is the electronic form in which the cleaning service is evaluated by giving scores to the inspected building together with the Cleaning Services unit and cleaning company officials.
