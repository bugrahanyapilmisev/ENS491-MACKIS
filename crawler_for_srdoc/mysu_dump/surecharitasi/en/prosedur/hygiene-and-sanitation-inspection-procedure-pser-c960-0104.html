Hygiene and Sanitation Inspection Procedure (PSER-C960-0104)

Procedure No
PSER-C960-0104V11
Effective Date
17-04-2012
Update Date
30-04-2025
Related Unit / Owner
SER / Hizmetler Direktörlüğü
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Related Instruction
Food and Beverage Services Provision Instruction Letter (ISER- C960-01)
Aim
Measuring the cleanliness of the operating areas of the companies that provide catering services in the University Center and tea-making services on the SU campus.
Scope
Food, personnel, equipment, storage, preparation and general area inspection.
Definitions / Abbreviations

SU: Sabancı University

SER: Services Directorate
FBS: Food and Beverage Services is the department responsible for supervising the service of the company that provides food and beverage services at Sabancı University, and meeting additional requests regarding the subject.

Firm: The firm(s) responsible for the provision of food and beverage services within the framework of the agreements made with Sabancı University.

Implementation Steps

Responsibility

Outputs/Timing

Hygiene and Sanitation Inspection with Scoring:

Inspection of the firm’s operation field in line with the Hygiene and Sanitation Inspection with Scoring Form (FSER-C960-0104) filling out the form. If the firm does not meet the requirements specified in the Technical Specifications, penalties will be applied.
