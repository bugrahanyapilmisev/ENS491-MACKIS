Aim
The aim of this procedure is to define the implementation steps for renting SU-owned spaces to third parties for fundraising purposes, and to book the revenues generated from these rentals after collection, identifying the responsible parties, the timing of the process, and the outputs. If SU-owned spaces serve any purpose that benefits SU students and staff, they may be rented free of charge, subject to the evaluation and approval of the university administration. In such a case, the purpose is to ensure the management of the relevant process.
Scope
This procedure covers all the steps that must be implemented during the process of renting SU-owned spaces for fundraising purposes, including planning, sales, tracking the results, and generating information for the following year.
