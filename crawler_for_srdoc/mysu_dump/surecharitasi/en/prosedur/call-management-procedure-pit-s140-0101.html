Related Instruction
Aim
To ensure that support requests from users are recorded and forwarded to the relevant support units.
Scope
This procedure covers the recording of support services needed during the use of the services and facilities provided by University Units and the forwarding of these to the relevant support units.
Definitions / Abbreviations

SU: Sabancı University

IT: Information Technologies

Suticket: Call recording/tracking application

Call: User support request

1. Call Recording

    

1.1. SU users submit their problems or service requests to IT through the following Call Recording/Tracking application.  

   SUTicket : https://suticket.sabanciuniv.edu  

  SU Users  SUTicket 

2. Call Resolution

    

2.1. Calls received from users are resolved by the relevant team in the IT department, depending on the content of the call. The user is informed with the necessary explanations. 

IT Help Desk Officer

Solution, Explanation / Call Resolution

2.2. Software issues are supported remotely via the remote connection application (remote access management is not used for upper management requests). Hardware support for laptops is provided to the IT Support Center, and the laptop is brought to the Support Center by the user. For desktop computers that cannot be transported, hardware troubleshooting is performed at the computer's location, and repairs and replacements are carried out at the IT Support Center.

IT Help Desk Officer

 Solution Provision Method/

2.3. The resolution details are recorded in Suticket, and the user is notified via email, and the call is closed.

 IT Help Desk Officer

5. Call Escalation

    
5.1. Calls from end users regarding malfunctions and problems are recorded as Incident calls and, whenever possible, are escalated to the IT end-user support team.

Call center officer / IT Helpdesk officer 

 Incident call type /
5.2. Calls for issues that the IT end-user technical support team cannot provide support for are escalated to the relevant IT department expert teams.

Call center officer / IT Helpdesk officer

Call record / Immediately
5.3. Calls escalated within the IT department are distributed, resolved, and closed according to the expert level.

IT Helpdesk officer

Call assignment / Within resolution time

6. Reporting / Survey

  
  

6.1. Unit reports for calls are prepared and published in the BI Reporting environment.

Reporting is typically created as follows:

  • Number of resolved calls by operator - Monthly
  • Average call resolution time by operator - Monthly
  • Total number of IT calls opened - Monthly
  • Number of opened calls by service - Monthly
  • List of users who opened the most calls - Monthly
  • Number of calls exceeding the SLA - Monthly

Call center officer

Preparation of reports / Relevant unit reporting period

6.2. The institutional development unit conducts a general satisfaction survey of university service units using the NPS (Net Promoter Score) method monthly for all administrative and academic users and eight times a year for students. Based on the results, additional surveys may be conducted to improve the quality of support services, if necessary.

 Related units

Survey / Within the year

6.3. Root cause analysis is conducted for recurring calls, and permanent solutions are developed.
