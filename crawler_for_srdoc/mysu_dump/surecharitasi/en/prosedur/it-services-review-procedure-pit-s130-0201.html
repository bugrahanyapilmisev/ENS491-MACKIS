IT Services Review Procedure (PIT-S130-0201)

Procedure No
PIT-S130-0201-V10
Effective Date
26-04-2011
Update Date
12-06-2024
Related Unit / Owner
IT / Bilgi Teknolojileri Direktörü
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Related Instruction
Bilgi Teknolojileri Olanaklarını Planlama Yönergesi (IIT-S110-01)
Aim
To review the services provided by the Information Technology Unit and plan preventive actions for necessary improvements.
Scope
Information Technology Resources
Definitions / Abbreviations

GS: General Secretariat

IT: Information Technologies

TYK: IT Unit Technical Steering Committee

Service Manager: IT employee responsible for the service

 

 

Implementation Steps:

 Responsibility 

 Outputs/Timing

1. Service Review Activities

    

1.1. Products that have reached the end of their technological lifespan and for which support and warranty services cannot be provided are identified.

 Related IT

Manager

List of end-of-life products / Annually before budget preparation

1.2. In accordance with the IT Information Security policy, a third-party expert firm conducts a security audit of existing measures.

 Related IT

Manager

Security audit / Annually in May

1.3. Administrative and academic staff are surveyed annually regarding the following services.

  • IT Support Services (Academic and Administrative)
  • MySU Satisfaction Survey (Academic and Administrative)
  • Educational Technologies and Support Services (Academic)
  Related IT

Manager

  

Satisfaction surveys / Annually in April

1.4. An analysis of the support provided to the IT Help Desk through various means throughout the year is conducted (resolution times, complaints, disruptions, etc.)

  Related IT

Manager

Analysis of IT Help Desk support services / Monthly

1.5. Disaster preparedness procedures are tested at least annually in a drill to identify potential disruptions.

 Related IT

Manager

Disaster drill and assessment / Annually 

1.6. Planned and unplanned outage records for critical systems and services are reviewed (Internet Service, Email Services, and corporate applications, etc.)

  Related IT

Manager

Outage records / January, April, July, October

1.7.External companies are evaluated as specified in the Outsourced Services Management (PIT-S130-0108) procedure.

  Related IT

Manager

 Service level report / Annually before budget preparation

2. Preparation of the Annual Operating Plan and Evaluation of Preventive Activities

    

2.1. The information, documents, and preventive activities obtained from the activities mentioned in Article 1 are compiled in the Annual Operating Plan (FIT-S11001-04). These are reviewed at the Board of Directors meetings.

IT Managers

 Review report/January, April, July, October

2.2. The identified preventive activities are entered into the Corrective/Preventive Actions Form (FID-S62004-01)

  Related Service 

Officer

Preventive Actions Form (FID-S62004-01)/After determining corrective and preventive actions

 

 

2.3. In cases where resources are needed for preventive activities, the identified activity is evaluated within the scope of the following year's operating plan. In cases where resources are not required, these are handled within the framework of the IT Call Management Procedure (PIT-S140-0101)

 

 Related IT Manager

 

Review report/January, April, July, October

 

2.4. Areas for improvement are identified based on discussions with GS regarding the plan. The IT Unit Annual Operating Plan (FIT-S11001-04) is updated along with planned activities and resource allocation, if necessary.

  Related IT Manager 

IT Unit annual operating plan update/After budget preparations
