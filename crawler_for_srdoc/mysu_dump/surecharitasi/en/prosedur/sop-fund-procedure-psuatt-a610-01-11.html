Aim
The aim of this fund is to support the research and education activities of the Faculty of Engineering and Natural Sciences.
Scope
This procedure covers the implementation steps for managing the Industry-Focused Projects Program fund established to support the research and education activities of the Faculty of Engineering and Natural Sciences.
Definitions / Abbreviations

SU: Sabancı University

FENS: Faculty of Engineering and Natural Sciences

MI: Directorate of Financial Affairs

SUATT: Directorate of Research, Development and Technology Transfer Office

INSTITUTION: Public or industrial organization that is a potential or joint project implementer participating in the Industry-Focused Projects Program

PAF: Project Research Fund

SOP: ENS 491 and ENS 492 Graduation Project I and II are undergraduate courses in the Industry-Focused Projects Program. These projects involve educational projects conducted on research topics related to the industry institution, and are led by fourth-year undergraduate students, faculty members, and institutions.

2. SOP Payment Process

2.1. The project partner institution can support the FENS's education and research activities with a cash contribution on a voluntary basis. Contributions can be made on behalf of the institution, or individuals can contribute. If the institution will pay against invoices, an INDUSTRY-FOCUSED PROJECTS PROGRAM PROTOCOL is signed between SU, Inovent, and the institution. The protocol specifies the regulation regarding Intellectual Property Rights.
