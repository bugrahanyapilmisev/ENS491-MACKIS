Instruction Letter for Study Visa and Residence Permit (ISR-C210-05)

Instruction No
IIRO-C430-02
Effective Date
15-12-2013
Update Date
02-11-2020
Related Unit / Owner
IRO / Uluslararası İlişkiler Ofisi Direktörlüğü
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Aim
To explain the rules for foreign students who have the right to register or continue their education at Sabancı University to obtain a residence permit.
Scope
This directive covers foreign students who have the right to enroll in Sabancı University and foreign students who continue their education.
Principle
Foreign students who have the right to register or continue their education at Sabancı University must obtain a Residence Permit and renew it before its expiration in order for their registration to be valid.
Statutory Basis

Higher Education Law No. 2547

Definitions / Abbreviations

Visa: The visa document required to enter the country, if necessary.
Residence Permit: The document issued by the Provincial Directorate of Migration Management, necessary for foreign students to stay in Turkey during a certain period of time.
SR: Student Resources Unit
SU: Sabancı University
IRO: International Relations Office
Foreign student: Student who is studying in educational institutions in Turkey and doesn’t have Turkish nationality or blue card.
YU: Foreign ID Number
Provincial Directorate of Migration Management: The official institution to which foreign students apply to obtain or extend a residence permit.

Regulations

Rules

1. VISA
1.1. The foreign students accepted to study in higher education institutions in Turkey may enter the country legally with any suitable type of visa. They are not obliged to get a student visa. Type of visa determined by the Embassy/Consulate of the Republic of Turkey in the country where the foreign students come from.
1.2. If the students come from countries that have visa exemption agreement between Turkey, does not need to take any visa.
2. RESIDENCE PERMIT
2.1. The Residence Permit Card is in the form of a student ID card and is issued by the Provincial Directorate of Migration Management if the application is completed.
2.2. Foreign students are required to apply for a residence permit during the visa / visa exemption period.
2.3. The foreign student who completes the registration process at the university fills in the online application form on http://e-ikamet.goc.gov.tr web address. Then, the student submits the necessary documents (student certificate, passport photocopy, address declaration form, biometric photo, residence card fee receipt) and online application form to IRO in 5 (five) working days.
2.4. The foreign student submits a copy of the application form to the SR.
2.5. The IRO submits the residence application files of foreign students to the Provincial Directorate of Migration Management with a delivery report.
2.6. IRO submits a copy of the signed delivery report to the SR.
2.7. The information of foreign students whose application for residence is rejected or put on hold due to lack of documents, visa violation or other reasons is sent to the IRO by the Provincial Directorate of Migration Management.
2.8. IRO guides foreign students whose applications are rejected for visa violation or other reasons to apply to the Provincial Directorate of Migration Management.
2.9. In case the application for residence is rejected, the foreign student must apply to the Provincial Directorate of Migration Management in person.
2.10. IRO helps foreign students complete their missing documents and submit them to the Provincial Directorate of Migration Management.
2.11. IRO assists students whose residence card has not been mailed although their application is accepted, sent to the wrong address and have problems with the card after the application process.
2.12. Students who are leaving Turkey before receiving residence card, should carry the approval document showing that the application for residence has been approved. This document is given by the Provincial Directorate of Migration Management. It is the responsibility of the student in this situation to go to the Provincial Directorate of Migration Management at least five days before their travel and obtain the approval document.
2.13. After obtaining the Residence Permit, a double-sided copy of the card is sent by the student to the SR to be stored in the student file.
2.14. YU ID number is a number shown as “Foreign Identity Number” on the residence permit card. Until the residence permit arrives, Student Resources register the student's information into their own information system, then this information is transferred to YÖKSİS (Higher Education Institution Student Information System) and the students' YÖK number is created. Students make their Istanbul transportation card applications with this temporary number until the YU number arrives.
2.15. Foreign Identity Number must be notified to the SR because it is stated on important documents such as student certificate and diploma.
2.16. Students whose residence permit duration is less than 60 days, receive a reminder e-mail by SR to make an extension application.
2.17. IRO provides the necessary process and document information to the students who need to apply for an extension.
2.18. In order to make extension, students must submit the required documents to the IRO after filling the application form at http://e-ikamet.goc.gov.tr at earliest 59 days or at least 1 day before the residence permit expiration date.
2.19. IRO submits the residence extension application files of foreign students to the Provincial Directorate of Migration Management with a delivery report, then submits a copy of the signed delivery report to the SR.
2.20. Foreign students, who lost their residence permit cards, are obliged to go to the nearest police station to get the lost report and apply to the Immigration Administration with this report and get a new card.
2.21. The residence permit card becomes invalid as of graduation date. Graduates must leave Turkey within 10 days from that date or they may apply for a new residence in accordance with the purpose of the stay. It is the responsibility of the individual to apply for a resident after graduation. Students are informed by the SR that their residence cards will be canceled as of their graduation date.
2.22. The residence permit of the student who cannot graduate before the normal study period is extended for a maximum of one year, not to exceed the maximum study period.
2.23. The residence permit of the student who leaves the university or freezes semester is canceled as of the date of leaving or freezing. The students in this situation are informed by the SR that their residence cards will be canceled.
3. IMPLEMENTATION
Since the information and rules specified in Article 2 may change due to the regulations in the applications, the students must follow all the current information on the official websites of the General Directorate of Migration Management and Sabancı University.
