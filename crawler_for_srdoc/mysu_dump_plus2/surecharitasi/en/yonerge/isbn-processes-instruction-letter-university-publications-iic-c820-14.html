ISBN Processes Instruction Letter for University Publications (IIC-C820-14)

Instruction No
IIC-C820-14V01
Effective Date
22-02-2022
Update Date
22-02-2022
Related Unit / Owner
IC / Bilgi Merkezi Direktörlüğü
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Aim
To determine the principles, definitions, and rules for works prepared by Sabancı University units and requested for publication, for which Sabancı University will be the publisher.
Scope
All works to be published by the university and for which an ISBN is required are within the scope of this document.
Principle
The rules defined in this Instruction Letter must be applied for all works requested for publication by Sabancı University units, for which an ISBN is required, and for which Sabancı University will be the publisher. An ISBN obtained by Sabancı University for one work cannot be used for another work by Sabancı University. If a work for which an ISBN has been previously issued is republished with changes to its content and format, a new ISBN is required. However, if the work is to be republished without any changes, a new ISBN is not required.
Statutory Basis
Definitions / Abbreviations

Work: All written material requiring an ISBN.

ISBN: The book numbering system known as the "International Standard Book Number," developed by the International Standardization Organization (ISO) and approved by member countries.

University: Sabancı University.

Suform: Sabancı University's online request/approval collection system.

Bandrol: A holographic security label or digitally generated security label containing a security strip that breaks when removed and destroys the material to which it is affixed. The label is used to prevent unauthorized reproduction and imitation of intellectual and artistic works.

IC: Sabancı University Information Center

Regulations

1. For a work requiring an ISBN, the unit requesting publication of the work in question must complete the ISBN Request Form via Suform (Suform - New Form - Information Center - ISBN Request Form). The ISBN request process is initiated by uploading the document containing the requested information to the system.

2. After first filling out the form via Suform and obtaining approval from the requesting person's director, and then from the Vice President responsible for Institutional Affairs and Social Impact, the Information Center initiates the ISBN process.

3. Following the Information Center's receipt of the ISBN, the necessary notifications are sent to the unit initiating the Suform process via Suform and, if necessary, via email.

4. Using the ISBN number received by the unit initiating the Suform process, the applicant unit completes the bandrol procedures detailed below:

• Applications are made through the General Directorate of Copyright's online system, "Bandrol Application Screen."

• The relevant documents are prepared and all documents are hand-delivered to YAYFED in a blue folder:

  • Türkiye Vakıflar Bankası payment – ​​2 payment receipts
  • Signed "Application Petition"
  • Signed "Authorization Certificate" for the delivery of the stamped documents
  • Authorized Use of Financial Rights Permission Document signed by the author
  • "Printing Office Letter" signed by the printing company

• Finally, the stamped documents received from YAYFED and a photocopy of the undertaking are sent to the printing house.

 The completed stamped document is sent to the printing house for printing.

5. The unit initiating the Suform process is responsible for all notifications to the relevant official authorities, including the prosecutor's office, and for monitoring the process after printing. Six copies of the publications must be sent to the Compilation Directorate, one copy to the ISBN agency, and two copies to the IC.

6. The responsibility for delivering printed works for which the ISBN number has been obtained, but copies of which have not been delivered within the specified period, as specified in notifications from official authorities, such as the Compilation Directorate, that control and monitor ISBN registrations, rests with the unit that initiated the Suform process.

7. Lists and information about works for which ISBNs have been obtained are forwarded by the Information Center to the requesting University unit for inclusion in reports requested by official authorities.
