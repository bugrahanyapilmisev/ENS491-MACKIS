Aim
The aim of this procedure is to pre-inspect and accept purchased materials required by Sabancı University for its operations, ensuring their internal delivery to relevant parties; to initiate the procurement process, storage, and inventory control of spare parts and materials held in stock under appropriate conditions; and to process and record fixed asset transactions for designated materials purchased related to Information Technology.
Scope
This procedure covers the processes related to purchased non-stock materials required by all relevant units within Sabancı University, as well as consumables, spare parts, and printed documents determined by Procurement and Operations, Food and Beverage Services, Cleaning Services, Health Services, Information Technologies, the General Secretariat, Student Resources, the Information Center, and the School of Languages.

Stocked material quantities are controlled visually and through reports automatically generated by the SAP-MM Module. Waste Management/Waste Disposal:

Waste is disposed of according to the designated holding period or the occupancy rate of the waste site. For this purpose, the waste site is regularly inspected.

Waste Management Officers notify the relevant Department Technical Officer of any leaks or spills at the waste site.

The steps in the Chemical Spill Response Instructions (FPOP-S220-01-10) are followed.

In the context of major environmental accidents, the Emergency Procedure (PPOP-S210-01-02) is applied.
