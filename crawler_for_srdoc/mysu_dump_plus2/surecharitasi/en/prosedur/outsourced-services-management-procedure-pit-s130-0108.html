Outsourced Services Management Procedure (PIT-S130-0108)

PIT-S130-0108V14

13-01-2012

Related Instruction
Bilgi Teknolojileri İşlerini Yönetme Yönergesi (IIT-S130-01)
Aim
To define the process steps for managing and monitoring outsourced services to develop and maintain IT services.
Scope
Outsourced services are covered by this procedure.
Definitions / Abbreviations

IT: Information Technology
Service Manager: IT employee responsible for the outsourced service

 Implementation Steps Responsibility

Outputs/Timing

1. Service Procurement

    

1.1. The procurement method for the activities envisioned in the IT unit&#39;s annual operating plan is determined. 

IT Managers,

General Secretary

Annual operating plan / Before budget studies

1.2. The service manager initiates the process for the services to be outsourced in accordance with University guidelines and procedures. 

Service Manager

Purchase request / Within the framework of the annual operating plan.

1.3. A service contract, including technical service levels, is created within the framework of the University&#39;s procurement and legal department rules.

 

Procurement Unit,Legal, IT Managers

Contract and procurement / After approval

1.4. The service manager creates relevant inventory records.

 Service Manager Inventory records / After procurement

2. Service Monitoring and Review

    

2.1. Service Managers perform checks on inventory records to monitor service levels and prepare reports prior to budget preparation.

 Service Manager

Service level assessment report / Annually before budget preparation

2.2. Service reviews are conducted in accordance with the rules established by the University&#39;s procurement department. Procurement of Goods and Services Procedure (FGS-S730-0101)

 Service Manager

Supplier Evaluation Form (FGS-S73001-03)
