Procedure For Preparation Of Instruction Letters and Directions and Procedures For Student-Focused Operational Processes (PSR-C270-0201)

Procedure No
PSR-C270-0201-V04
Effective Date
21-03-2005
Update Date
21-12-2023
Related Unit / Owner
SR / Öğrenci Kaynakları Direktörü
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Related Instruction
Aim
It is to explain the implementation steps regarding the preparation and revision of the academic Instruction Letters regarding education and training and the instructions and procedures regarding the operational processes under the responsibility of the Student Resources Unit.
Scope
It covers students, academic and administrative units.
Definitions / Abbreviations

AC: Academic Council

GSO: Office of the Secretary General

SR: Student Resources Unit

VP-E: Vice President Responsible for Education

SU: Sabancı University

YOK: Presidency of the Higher Education Council

Application Steps:

Responsibility

Outputs / Timing

1. Revision of Instruction Letters:

 

 

1.1. The SR finalizes the instruction letter revision suggestions by coordinating with the relevant academic and administrative units due to the current instruction letters not meeting the needs or changes in YOK's education-related legislation.

SR

Preparation of Instruction Letter Change Suggestions / When the need for revision of the instruction letters arises

 

1.2. The SR receives the opinions of the VP-E in case of new and radical application suggestions that will affect the legislation within the scope of legal instruction letters regarding education and training. By coordinating with the relevant academic and administrative units, reflecting the revision suggestions to the instruction letter, the Legal Unit obtains its opinion and prepares it for submission to the approval of the AC.

SR

Preparation of Instruction Letter Change Suggestions for Submission to EC Approval/ Following the preparation of instruction letter amendment proposals

1.3. The SR organizes the AC meeting and adds instruction letter revision suggestions to the meeting agenda.

SR

Organization of the AC Meeting / At the stage of creating agenda items

1.4. The AC either approves the instruction letter revision proposals as they are or makes a decision by expressing its opinions and suggestions. The revision suggestions are submitted to the Board of Trustees for approval.

AC

AC Decision / at the AC meeting

1.5. The SR sends the instruction letter revision proposals approved by the Board of Trustees to the General Directorate of Law and Legislation of the Presidency of Administrative Affairs to be published in the Official Gazette. In case new instruction letters regarding instruction letter revisions are requested, the SR completes the instruction letters by providing the necessary coordination within the University.

SR

Submission of Instruction Letter Amendments for Publication in the Official Gazette / After the approval of the Board of Trustees

1.6. The University Legal Advisor follows the Official Gazette and informs SR on the day the instruction letter changes are published. After the instruction letter changes come into force, the SR contacts the relevant units to update the existing instruction letters on the internal and external web.

University Legal Advisor / SR

 

Announcement of Instruction Letter Changes and Entry into Force / In the week it was published in the Official Gazette

2. Preparation or Revision of Directives and Procedures for Student-Focused Operational Processes:

 

 

2.1. The SR receives the opinions of the VP-E in case of new and radical application suggestions that will affect the legislation within the scope of legal instruction letters regarding education and training. It creates drafts or updates of directives and procedures, taking into account the opinions and suggestions of relevant academic and administrative units.

SR

Preparation of Directive and Procedure Drafts or Updates / Where necessary

2.2. The SR obtains the opinion of the Legal Unit regarding newly created or updated directives. The finalized directives and procedures are forwarded to the GSO to complete the approval process.

SR /

GSO

Submission of Directives and Procedures to GSO / After drafts or updates of directives and procedures are created

2.3. The GSO makes the necessary arrangements on the Student Focused Operational Processes web pages for the publication of the approved directives and procedures.

SR /

GSO

Announcement of Directives and Procedures / Within 3 business days following the approval of the President and Secretary General
