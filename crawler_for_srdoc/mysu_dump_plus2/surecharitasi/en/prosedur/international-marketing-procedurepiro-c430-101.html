Related Instruction
International Marketing Regulations (IIRO-C430-01)
Aim
The objective of this procedure is to state implementing steps regarding marketing activities and services for the potential international full time students of SU.
Scope
This procedure covers the marketing of SU in the relevant country/region to potential international students, school councelors and the partners and the services to the students who registered to the undergraduate/graduate programs.
Definitions / Abbreviations

IRO : International Relations Office

MC: Ministery of Commerce
Promotional Material : All types of printed and digital communications tool kit used in annual marketing activity.

2.    Implementation of International Marketing Plan

 

 

2.1.  International/off-campus events are held within the scope of the marketing plan. Marketing is carried out by visiting high schools, private teaching institutions and other social stakeholders (Embassy, office of education counselor, agency, institution, ministry, etc.) of the countries. International Education fairs are attended, seminars and competitions are held.

IRO

 Till the deadline of the online application period

2.2. On-campus marketing activities are held. Relevant persons and/or organizations (Educational organizations, educational agencies, prospective students and their families, councelors, etc.) convey their requests verbally or by email, stating the date they want to come to the campus, or the relevant organizations/people are invited to the campus and their visits to campus are organized (promotional presentations, food service in campus cafeteria, campus tour).

IRO

 All the year round

2.3. Marketing presentations are given to various stakeholders (such as higher education institutions foundations, undersecretaries, etc.) in the relevant countries; bilateral agreements are made and followed up with these stakeholders in coordination with the departments of SR and Law to ensure the education of full-time international students at SU.
