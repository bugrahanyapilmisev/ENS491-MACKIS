Aim
Performing the maintenance and operation of Sabancı University's SAP systems.
Scope
This procedure covers the maintenance and operation of SAP systems for which infrastructure services are provided by the Information Technologies (IT) Unit on behalf of the University.
Definitions / Abbreviations

SU: Sabancı University

IT: Information Technology

Unit: Sabancı University academic and administrative units

Unit Module Manager: The units SAP staff member responsible for SAP regulations

Company: SAP consulting firm

ÇTU: Call tracking application
