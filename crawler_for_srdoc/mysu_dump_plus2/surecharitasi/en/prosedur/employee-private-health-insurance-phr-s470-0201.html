Related Instruction
Employee Private Health Insurance
Aim
The steps to be followed in the inclusion of Sabancı University employees and their dependents (spouse and children) within the scope of Private Health Insurance are explained.
Scope
Applies to full-time employees and their dependents (spouse and children) of the University. Detailed information on the scope and use of Health Insurance is provided on the MYSU / Human Resources / Health Insurance web page.
Definitions / Abbreviations

HR = Human Resources

FA = Finance and Accounting

Imlementation Steps   

 Responsibility 

 Outputs/Timing 

1. FOR NEW RECRUITS

 

 

1.1. The private health insurance application document is sent to the person with the job start package documents list.

HR

Health Insurance Application Document / Starting Day of Work

1.2.The person fills out the private health insurance application document completely and submits it to the HR Unit on the day they start work.

Important Note:

Employees and their Dependents must fill out the application document completely and state their Turkish Republic Identity Number in order to enter health insurance. Foreign national employees and their dependents must enter with a Foreign Identity Number. Those who have not obtained this identity number must provide their Tax Identity Number. (Foreign Identity Number can be obtained after obtaining a work-residence permit. Those who do not have a Foreign Identity Number must obtain a Tax Identity Number from any tax office in order to complete the entry procedures.) Otherwise, health insurance entries cannot be made.

New Recruit

Completed Health Insurance Application Document / Starting Day of Work

1.3. If the person to be insured has previously under another private health insurance, and if the new policy is changed without a break within 30 days, the acquired rights will be transferred, so a photocopy of the old insurance certificate must be declared to the health insurance company.

New Recruit

Old Health Insurance Certificate Photocopy / In the first days of work

1.4. The collected documents are sent to the broker company by e-mail by the HR unit for the insurance entry to be made.

HR

By Mail / When the documents are completed

1.5. If the Insurance Company deems it necessary, it sends a letter requesting a medical report from the person regarding past illnesses to the employee through the broker company.

Insurer

Report Request Letter / After the application is evaluated by the Insurance Company

1.6. The employee sends the report requested by the insurance company regarding past illnesses to the insurance company through the broker company.

Employee 

Doctor's Report / When the report is received

1.7. The Health Insurance Company evaluates the person's application and informs the employee by e-mail after the insurance entry is made.

Insurer

Insurance Policy / 1-2 Weeks After the Application

1.8. The HR Unit sends the addendums received from the Health Insurance Company to the FA Unit.

HR

Insurance Addendum / When the Insurance Company Forwards it to the HR Department

2. FOR NEWLY MARRIED EMPLOYEES

 

 

2.1 In order for the spouse to be included in the insurance, the employee must document the marriage status and submit the health insurance application document, a copy of the marriage certificate, a copy of the spouse's ID card and, if the surname has changed, a copy of his/her own ID card to the HR unit within 30 days of the marriage date at the latest.

Employee

Health Insurance Application Document / Marriage Certificate Photocopy and ID Card Photocopies / After Marriage

2.2. The collected documents are forwarded to the broker company by e-mail by the HR Unit.

HR

Sağlık Sigortası Başvuru Belgesi / Evlilik Cüzdanı Fotokopisi ve Nüfus Cüzdan Fotokopileri / Evraklar Tamamlandığında

2.3. If deemed necessary, the Insurance Company sends a letter requesting a medical report from the employee's spouse regarding the past illnesses specified in the application document to the employee through the broker company.

Insurer

Report Request Letter / After the Application is Evaluated by the Insurance Company

2.4. The employee sends the report requested by the insurance company regarding the past illnesses of his/her spouse to the insurance company through the broker company.

Employee

Doctor's Report / When the Report is Received

2.5. The Health Insurance Company evaluates the application and informs the employee by e-mail after making the insurance entry.

 

 

2.6.The HR Unit sends the addendums received from the Broker Company to the FA Unit

HR

Insurance Addendum / When the Insurance Company Forwards it to the HR Department

3. FOR EMPLOYEES WITH NEWBORN CHILDREN

 

 

3.1. In order for the newborn child to be included in the insurance, the employee shall submit the health insurance application document, the birth report received from the hospital, and the child's identity document to the HR unit within 30 days of the date of birth at the latest.

Employee

Health Insurance Application Document / Birth Report and ID Card Copy / As soon as the baby is born

3.2. The collected documents are forwarded to the broker company by e-mail by the HR Unit.

HR

Health Insurance Application Document / Birth Report and ID Card Copy / When the Documents are Completed

3.3. The Health Insurance Company evaluates the application and informs the employee by e-mail after entering the insurance.

Insurer

Insurance Policy / 1-2 Weeks After Application

3.4. The addendum sent to the HR Unit from the Broker Company is sent to the FA Unit.

HR

Insurance Addendum / When the Insurance Company Forwards it to the HR Department

4. FOR EMPLOYEES WHO LEAVE THE JOB

 

 

4.1. The HR Unit sends the information about the personnel who will leave the job and any dependents to the broker company via e-mail.

HR

Leaving letter / On the last working day
     

4.2. . The addendum sent to the HR Unit from the Broker Company is sent to the FA Unit.

HR

Insurance Addendum / When the Insurance Company Forwards it to the HR Department
