Aim
To carry out the maintenance and operation of Sabancı University's CRM systems.
Scope
This procedure covers the maintenance and operation of CRM systems for which infrastructure services are provided by the Information Technologies (IT) Unit on behalf of the University.
Definitions / Abbreviations

SU: Sabancı University

IT: Information Technology

Suticket: Call recording/tracking application

Unit: Sabancı University academic and administrative units
