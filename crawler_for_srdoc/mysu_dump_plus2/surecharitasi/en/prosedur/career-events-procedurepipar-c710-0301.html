Career Events Procedure(PIPAR-C710-0301)

Procedure No
PIPAR-C710-0301-V07
Effective Date
10-04-2012
Update Date
12-06-2024
Related Unit / Owner
IPAR / Kurumsal İşbirlikleri ve Mezunlarla İlişkiler
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Related Instruction
Kariyer Geliştirme Yönergesi (IPAR-C710-03)
Aim
The aim of this procedure is to provide guidance on the application of rules regarding the preparation and organization of Career Events.
Scope
Definitions / Abbreviations

IPAR: Institutional Partnerships and Alumni Relations

CO: Career Office

AO: Alumni Office

MIC: Marketing and Institutional Communications Unit

SÜMED: Sabancı University Alumni Association

EBC: Economics and Business Club

IES: Industrial Engineers Society

Procedure Steps

Responsibility

 Outputs/Timing

1. Career Events

    

1.1 Events for Professional Life

    
On-Campus Recruitment Days (OCR)
    
A poster is prepared to actively announce the event to students; it is announced on the internal web and social media. CO & MIC & Contact Center Event Posters and Visuals / February - March
An invitation is sent to the list of institutions prepared by the Career Office.  CO OCR Invitation/ February - March
Institutions that want to participate in the event send a Planning Request for OCR from our career platform. Participating Institutions  OCR Institution Registration/ February - March
Room reservations are made according to the days institutions would like to attend and availability of the rooms. (Suform)   CO Room Reservation / March
Institutions evaluate the applications from students and send interview invitations to students they find positive via the platform before the interview date, and students who receive invitations register via the platform. Participating Institutions Inviting Students/Prior to the event
On the day of the event, companies that come to the campus are welcomed and directed to the interview rooms.   CO  OCR / Prior to the event
Institutions conduct job and/or internship interviews with the students they invite at the specified times. Participating Institutions OCR /During the event
The online “Campus Interview Days Evaluation Form” is sent to the institutions participating in the event. (Campus Interview Days Evaluation Form - FIPAR-C71003-02)   CO 
 OCR Evalution Form / April- May
At the end of the event, a newsletter is prepared and sent to MIC and the same bulletin is also published on the Career web page. CO & MIC OCR Press release / May
The event photos/poster are sent to the Information Center to be archived. (University History Special Collection Information Source Transfer Form -FIC-C86001-01) CO & Information Center Events Photos/ At the end of the term
Institution/Organization Info Meetings
    

 

Institutions and organizations may organize various events on campus or online at different times throughout the year to promote themselves;

 

  •  They can introduce their organizations by setting a stand on campus
  • They can introduce the sectors they are in with promotional presentations
  • They can share their experiences with students through activities where their managers participate as panelists
  • They can organize recruitment interviews and mock interviews
 Participating Institutions On-Campus Career Events/ / All year round
For institutions that will organize on-campus events, suitable dates are determined for students and institutions.   CO  Event Calendar/ / All year round
Class reservations are made for the determined suitable dates and the required audio and video systems are specified in the form. (Suform)    CO  Room reservartion/ Prior to the event
Events are announced through the internal web, career website, social media and student clubs.    CO & Student Clubs  Event Announcement / Prior to the event
Event photos are taken during the event.  CO  Career Event / During the event

Institutions can organize various events at different times throughout the year to promote themselves;

  • Factory, company tours,
  • Dinner organizations organized for high-potential students
 Institutions to realize the event  Off-Campus Career Events/ All year round
Appropriate dates are determined for institutions that will organize career events off-campus CO & Institutions to realize the event  Event Calendar / All year round
Event announcements are announced through the internal web, social media, career website and student clubs. CO & Student Clubs Event Announcement / Prior to the event
Applications are received from students who want to participate in the events. CO & Institutions to realize the event Participant List / Prior to the event
A shuttle service is arranged by the institution or CO for student transfers to the area where the events will be held in case of demand, and transportation information is shared with participating students. (Suform) KO & Etkinliği Geçekleştirecek Kurumlar Transportation Details / Prior to the event
Case Studies
    
The case studies event is a joint event organized with EBC.  CO & EBC  Case Studies / All year round
A joint event time is determined with companies that want to conduct a case study and EBC. CO & EBC
 Event Guest List / Beginning of Academic Term
Class reservations are made and the required audio and video systems are specified in the form (Suform). CO & EBC Room Reservation / Prior to the event
An event announcement is prepared and announced on the career website, social media, internal website and through student clubs. Participating Institution & CO & EBC Event Announcement / Prior to the event
Applications are collected from students who want to participate in the event and an informational email is sent regarding the event details. EBC Event Participant List/ Prior to the event
During the event, event photos are taken. CO & EBC Case Studies/ During the event
The event photos are sent to the Information Center for archiving. (University History Special Collection Information Resource Transfer Form (FIC-C86001-01) CO & Information Center Event Photos / At the end of the academic term
Alumni Panels
    
The Career Office organizes alumni panels with IES. CO & IES  Alumni Panels / Throughout the year
To plan the event, the topics and dates of the panels to be organized by the IES Board are determined. IES  Event Calendar / Throughout the year
A list of graduates working in fields related to the determined topics is obtained from the CO through the Career Office. CO & AO List of alumni to be invited / Prior to the event
The selected graduates are invited to the event with invitation email. IES Alumni Invitation / Prior to the event
The classroom reservation where the event will be held is made and the required audio and video systems are specified in the form. (Suform) CO & IES Room Reservation / Prior to the event
The event announcement is prepared; it is announced through the career website, internal website, social media and student clubs. CO & IES Event Announcement / Prior to the event
The event photos are sent to the Information Center to be archived. University History Special Collection Information Source Transfer Form (FIC-C86001-01) CO Event Photos / At the end of the academic term

1.1 Career Events for Academic Life

    
Graduate Degree Information Meetings
    
Students who have questions about their graduate degree university preferences make an appointment through the CO's calendar. CO  Appointment Calendar / Throughout the year
During face-to-face or online meetings, the steps to be followed while making university research/preference are explained and useful resources are shared with the students. CO Useful Resources / Throughout the year
Overseas University Info Events    
A suitable date is determined with institutions and/or university officials who want to organize an overseas university promotion CO Determination of date / Prior to the event
A suitable class reservation is made in line with the determined date and time and the required audio and visual systems are specified in the form. (Suform) CO Room reservation / Prior to the event
An event announcement is prepared and announced on the internal web, social media and career website, and shared with the officials of the relevant student clubs to be announced to the students. CO& Student Clubs Event Announcement / Prior to the event
During the event, event photos are taken. CO University Promotions/ During the event
2. . Career Counseling Service    
Students make an appointment to receive counseling service via the CO's calendar. Meeting duration is 30 minutes. CO CO Calendar / Throughout the academic year
The counseling appointment calendar can be accessed on the career website. CO CO Calendar / Throughout the academic year
After the counseling meetings, the Career Office Satisfaction Survey is sent to the student. CO Career Office Satisfaction Survey / After the meeting

3.Career Website and Career Link Platform

    
Event announcements are published on the Internal web and Career websites before each event. CO Etkinlik Duyuruları / Throughout the academic year
Education and scholarship announcements are published on the internal web and career website. CO Duyurular / Throughout the academic year
Job and internship advertisements are published on the Career Link platform. CO Career Link/ Throughout the academic year 
Ads published by institutions are checked and approved by the Career Office. CO Career Link / Throughout the academic year 
Institutions that publish advertisements collect applications via Career Link or their own websites and contact the students they choose. Institutions publishing the ad Applications / Throughout the academic year
