Procedure for Grade Conversion Process for Exchange Period (PIRO-C420-0104)

Değişim Programları Kapsamında Giden Öğrenci Yönergesi (IIRO-C420-01)
Aim
This procedure outlines the steps to be followed during and after the transfer of grades for Sabancı University undergraduate and graduate students who participate in exchange programs, ensuring that the courses taken at partner institutions are properly reflected in SU’s system.
Scope
This procedure applies to undergraduate and graduate students participating in exchange programs and covers the steps for converting grades obtained during the exchange period into the SU system and recognizing them within program requirements.
Definitions / Abbreviations

• IRO: International Relations Office
• Exchange Academic Advisor: Faculty member appointed by the student’s registered Graduate School Director to monitor academic progress, provide guidance according to student interests, and support course selection and add/drop. For outgoing students, also authorized to determine course equivalencies in the Learning Agreement.
• EC: Exchange Committee
• Faculty Administrative Board: Faculty Administrative Board of related Sabancı University faculties
• Faculty/Graduate School: The faculty or graduate school where the student is registered
• GPA: Grade Point Average
• Partner Institution: Institution with which SU has an exchange agreement
• ECTS: European Credit Transfer System
• LA: Learning Agreement
• SR: Student Resources
• SU: Sabancı University
• Transcript: Academic grade report

Application Steps

Responsibility

Outputs / Timeline

1.1.  Students are responsible for tracking their exchange program transcripts.

Student

When grades are released by the partner institution

1.2.  Transcripts/grade reports are provided by the partner institution, either as wet-signed and stamped or electronically approved documents..

Partner Institution

When grades are released

1.3. Students check the received documents and share them with IRO.

Student

Upon receipt from the partner institution

1.4. Grades received from the host institution are converted into the SU grading system according to conversion tables approved by the Academic Board and entered by SR.

SR

Grade entry / After IRO forwards documents to SRO

1.5. For universities and/or grade conversion tables not included in the Academic Board-approved conversion tables, IRO prepares a draft table based on the partner institution’s grading system, SU’s grading system, and the grading systems of the student’s host country, and shares it with DK members for feedback.

IRO

Preparation of new proposed conversion table

1.6. Draft grade conversion tables approved by EC are sent to the relevant faculties for evaluation and approval

IRO

Sending draft table to faculties / After DK approval

1.7. Faculty-approved grade conversion tables are submitted to the Academic Board for final approval via SR.

Faculties

After Faculty Board approval

1.8. The new grade conversion table is presented to the Academic Board.

SRO

After Faculty Board approval

1.9. If the Academic Board approves the grade conversion table, SR informs the relevant units. If not approved, feedback is shared with IRO for revision.

SRO, IRO

After Academic Board evaluation

1.10. Approved grade conversion tables are added to the existing system, and relevant units are informed.

IRO

After Academic Board approval
