Erasmus Programı Kapsamında Eğitim Alma Yönergesi (IIRO-C420-04)
Aim
This procedure defines the steps for Sabancı University staff participating in Erasmus+ Staff Training Mobility, including application, selection, mobility, and post-mobility steps.
Scope
Covers all application, selection, mobility, and post-mobility steps for Sabancı University staff participating in Erasmus+ Staff Training Mobility.
Definitions / Abbreviations
  • EU: European Union
  • SC: Selection Committee
  • SU: Sabancı University
  • NA: National Agency (Turkish Agency for EU Education and Youth Programs)
  • IRO: International Relations Office

 

Application Steps

Responsibility

Output / Timeline

1. Review of Evaluation Criteria

Evaluation criteria are reviewed based on the relevant year’s National Agency Implementation Handbook. Any updates by SC are submitted to the Rector for approval

 IRO/SC

 Evaluation / Before announcement

2. Announcement

 

 

2.1. IRO announces the Staff Training Mobility Call via erasmus@sabanciuniv.edu, IRO website, and the internal staff web page.

IRO

Call / As per IRO schedule

2.2. If applications are insufficient for the allocated budget, additional calls may be announced at alternative dates. Steps are repeated for each new call..

 IRO

New Call / As per IRO schedule

3. Application

 

 

3.1. Staff members learn the requirements, online application process, and necessary documents from the announcement.

Staff

During Call Period

3.2. Applications are submitted via the online application form. Staff complete the “Training Plan” with the partner institution, get partner approval, and upload it to the online form by the deadline.

Staff

Training Plan / By application deadline announced by IRO

4. Evaluation: IRO shares the list of eligible staff with SC. Applications are evaluated according to the announced criteria, and results are announced.

 

IRO, SC

Selection Decision / After application deadline

5. Notification of Selection Results: The IRO announces the names of the staff members selected to participate in the Staff Mobility for Training program, the institutions they will attend, those eligible for the Erasmus+ grant, and the amount of the grant, via the IRO website and email.

. 

IRO

Selection Results / After selection

6. Post-Selection

  

 

6.1. Following the announcement of the selection results, the IRO prepares the training grant agreement and ensures that all required signatures are obtained.

Staff/IRO

 Grant Agreement/ Following the announcement of the selected staff for the Training Mobility program

6.2. IRO enters mobility information into the European Commission Mobility Tool.

 IRO

 Report/ Following the announcement of the selected staff for the Training Mobility program

6.3. IRO opens a file for each selected staff member. File includes:

  • Training Plan (FIRO-C42004-01)
  • Erasmus+ Grant Agreement (FIRO-C42003-02)
  • Participation Certificate (FIRO-C42004-03)
  • Invitation Letter from partner institution
  • Travel Expense Declaration Form
  • Boarding Passes (or passport entry/exit stamp copies if lost)

IRO

Staff file/ Following the announcement of the selected staff for the Training Mobility program

7. Erasmus+ Grant Payments

 

 

7.1. Staff members with completed contracts are directed to complete the Travel Approval and Advance Payment Form online.

IRO

Grant Payments / Before departure

7.2. Staff requests the first installment of the grant (up to 80%) via the web form.

Staff

Grant Payments / Before departure

7.3. Following the submission of the duly approved web form to the Financial Affairs Unit, the payment will be made to the staff on the Friday preceding the week of their departure abroad.

Staff, IRO,

Finance

Grant Payments / Before departure

8. While Abroad 

    

   

8.1. Staff receives the partner-approved Participation Certificate after completing the training activity.

Staff

Participation Certificate / During mobility

9. After Return to SU

 

 

9.1. Staff prepares and submits the required documents to IRO:

  • Partner-approved Participation Certificate (FIRO-C42004-03)
  • European Commission Online Final Report
  • Travel documents (boarding passes)

Staff

Participation Certificate, Final Report, Travel documents,  / After returning to SU

9.2. The IRO reviews the Staff’s Certificate of Attendance and travel dates. If the participation report sent by the European Commission at the end of the mobility is completed and all documents are in order, the IRO calculates the remaining Erasmus+ grant amount based on the actual training period. The IRO then completes the Travel Expense Declaration Form and forwards it to the Financial Affairs Unit for the remaining payments to be processed.

IRO

Remaining Grant Payment, Travel Ticket Form / After returning to SU and document submission

9.3. Finance Unit transfers the remaining Erasmus+ grant to the staff member’s Euro account.

Finance Unit

Remaining Grant Payment / After returning to SU and document submission

9.4. IRO finalizes the staff file. File includes:

  • Training Plan
  • Erasmus+ Grant Agreement
  • Participation Certificate
  • Invitation Letter from partner institution
  • Travel Expense Declaration Form
  • Boarding Passes

IRO

Staff File/ Payment of remaining grant
