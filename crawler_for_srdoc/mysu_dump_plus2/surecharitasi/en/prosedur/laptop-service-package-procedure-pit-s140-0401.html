Aim
To describe the services provided for student laptops under the Laptop Service Package.
Scope
Laptops and users provided with computers under the Laptop Service Package are covered by this procedure.
Definitions / Abbreviations

Technical Support Center: The office that provides on-campus support for laptops.

Notification: Any alert or notification sent via fax, email, or associated application/website.

Equipment Delivery Document: A signed document that specifies the hardware and software specifications of the laptop, along with the terms and conditions of use.

IT: Information Technologies Unit

DBHP: Laptop Service Package

Application Steps (The following items are the rules that will apply to students who entered before the 2015-2016 academic year. Laptops will not be provided to students starting from the 2015-2016 academic year.)
