Aim
The aim of this instruction letter is to explain the rules and principles regarding the management and implementation of planned career activities.
Scope
This instruction letter covers career activities planned for professional business life and academic life.
Principle
Career activities at Sabancı University are carried out to support students in planning their professional and academic careers.
Statutory Basis
Definitions / Abbreviations

IPAR: Institutional Partnerships and Alumni Relations

CO: Career Office

AO: Alumni Office

MIC: Marketing and Institutional Communications Unit

SÜMED: Sabancı University Alumni Association

EBC: Economics and Business Club

IES: Industrial Engineers Society

Regulations

General

  1. Sabancı University Career events process is carried out by the CO, which is a part of the IPAR unit.
  2. The list of company contact information to be invited to Career Events is updated at the end of October each year.
  3. At the beginning of the relevant academic term each year, relevant student clubs (EBC, IES, etc.) are discussed and a joint event plan is created. The CO supports student clubs in career events.
  4. At the beginning of the academic term, a CO event calendar covering the entire year is prepared.
  5. Career Counseling meetings are held with students in need.
  6. Events to be held by the Career Office are announced on the internal web and social media accounts.
  7. Job postings from employers are announced to students via the internal web and in the job and internship posting system called Career Link, and job opportunities that interest graduates are forwarded to SÜMED to be shared with graduates.
  8. An online survey is conducted every two years to understand students' expectations from the CO. Expectations that stand out in line with the survey results are prioritized and an annual career event plan is created.

1. Career Events

Career events are held with 2 different contents; 

  • Career events for professional life
  • Career events for academic life

1.1. Career Events for Professional Life

These are events organized to facilitate students' move to professional life, develop their knowledge and skills, and introduce them to sectors and organizations suitable for the programs they will graduate from.

1.1.1 Campus Interview Days

A series of interview events held on campus every March-April to provide students with interview experience and meet the intern/new graduate needs of institutions.

1.1.2 Institution/Organization Info Events

Institutions/organizations organize various events such as presentations, stands and panels at different times throughout the year.

1.1.3. Case Studies

These are career events that bring institutions/organizations and students together in an interactive environment. Students who participate in Case Studies find solutions to the cases that institutions may encounter in real life. Case studies organized according to the demand from institutions/organizations throughout the year are conducted in partnership with EBC.

1.1.4. Alumni Panels

These are events where Sabancı University graduates working in different sectors share information about the sectors they work in, the organizations they work in, and the work they do with students on an interactive platform. These events are organized in partnership with IES.

1.2. Career Events for Academic Life:

Within the scope of career events for academic life, overseas university introductions and alumni panels are organized.

 

 

1.2.1. Graduate Degree Information Meetings

These are information sessions where steps to be followed while doing university research/choice are explained and useful resources are shared with students who want to do a master's degree. Interested students come to the meeting by making an appointment from the CO. If necessary, the CO directs students to CIAD - Academic Communication Unit during the information session.

1.2.2. Overseas University Info Meetings

These are presentations made by educational consultancy firms or overseas university officials about education opportunities, application conditions, and scholarship opportunities. 

1.2.3. Alumni Panels

Sabancı University graduates who are doing their master's or doctorate abroad share their knowledge and experiences with students who want to study abroad or are planning an academic career in a panel environment.  

2. Career Counseling

This is a one-on-one counseling service provided to students on career planning, resume and cover letter preparation, job and internship search strategies, and interview preparation, and is planned as 30-minute sessions.

3. Use of Career Website and Career Link

3.1. Career Website is a communication platform where information is shared by shedding light on students' academic and professional career planning, and where future events, scholarships, and education announcements are made.

3.2. Career Link is a platform where job and internship advertisements are published.
