Directive On Preparing Instruction Letters and Directives and Procedures For Student-Focused Operational Processes (ISR-C270-02)

ISR-C270-02
Effective Date

Related Unit / Owner
SR / Öğrenci Kaynakları Direktörlüğü

BT/ Board of Trustees
Aim
It explains the rules and principles of practice regarding the preparation and updating of the instruction letters containing general provisions regarding education and training, as well as the instructions and procedures for the operational processes under the responsibility of the Student Resources Unit.
Scope
It includes students, academic and administrative units, Academic Council, President and General Secretary.
Principle
It is essential that the instruction letters, directives and procedures are reliable in terms of integrity and up-to-dateness, and all relevant parties are required to comply with the rules specified in these documents.

Regulations

1. INSTRUCTION LETTERS

1.1. General Provisions:

a) The instruction letters that determine practices related to education and training and define the principles and rules related to them are prepared on the basis of YOK education and training legislation and instruction letters and come into force by being announced in the Official Gazette. 

b) The rules regarding education and student rights at SU are included in the instruction letters below and students are subject to the provisions of these instruction letters according to their level.

  • Basic Development Year Education and Training Instruction Letter
  • Undergraduate Education and Training Instruction Letter
  • Graduate Education and Training Instruction Letter

c) It is mandatory for students and academic and administrative units to comply with the practices described in the instruction letters.

ç) The instruction letters are published in Turkish and English on SU's external and internal web pages.

1.2. Revision of Instruction Letters:

a) The Announced Instruction Letters may be revised due to not meeting the needs during the implementation phase or due to changes in the education legislation and instruction letters of YOK.

b) In case of new and radical application proposals that may affect the legislation within the scope of legal instruction letters regarding education and training, the SR takes the opinions of the VP-E and coordinates with other academic and administrative units.

c) After adding the opinions of the Legal Unit to the revision suggestions, the instruction letter revision, which is finalized, is submitted for the approval of the AC and the Board of Trustees, respectively.

ç) Following the approval of the Board of Trustees, instruction letter revisions are sent to the General Directorate of Law and Legislation of the Presidency of Administrative Affairs to be published in the Official Gazette.

d) The instruction letter revisions enter into force on the date of publication in the Official Gazette and are published on the internal and external web.

 

2. DIRECTIVES AND PROCEDURES FOR STUDENT-FOCUSED OPERATIONAL PROCESSES

2.1. General Provisions:

a) The directives are the documents that define the rules and methods related to the work within the scope of the processes in question.

b) The procedures are the documents that are a continuation of instruction letters or directives, describing in detail the implementation steps, their responsibilities and outputs regarding the works within the scope of the processes in question.

c) The directives and procedures are published on SR's internal website.

2.2. Preparation and Revision of Directives and Procedures:

a) The new directives and procedures may be prepared in response to decisions taken by SU's decision-making bodies regarding student-focused operational processes or the need for a new instruction letter. The necessary coordination in this regard is provided by SR.

b) The announced directives and procedures may be revised if they do not meet the needs during the implementation phase, if the application changes or if new demands arise. The necessary coordination in this regard is provided by the SR.

c) In case of new and radical application suggestions that may affect the legislation within the scope of legal instruction letters regarding education and training, the SR takes the opinions of VP-E and ensures coordination with other academic and administrative units. Following the feedback from the units, the Legal Unit's opinion is taken for the guidelines. The finalized directives and procedures are submitted for the approval of the President and the Secretary General.

ç) The approved directives and procedures are published on SR's internal website.
