Transportation Services Instruction Letter (ISER-C930-01)

Instruction No
ISER-C930-01-V13
Effective Date
26-04-2000
Update Date
12-06-2024
Related Unit / Owner
SER / Hizmetler Direktörlüğü
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Aim
The aim of this instruction letter is to determine the principles and rules about meeting the transportation needs of Sabancı University staff members and students.
Scope
This instruction letter covers the regular personnel shuttles and shuttle service, which aims to meet the transportation needs of Sabancı University staff members and students and the employees of subcontractor companies determined by the University, and the special purpose vehicle requests of the units.
Principle
The principle is to meet the transportation needs of the Sabancı University community in the safest and best conditions.
Statutory Basis

Highway Traffic Law No. 2918, Road Transport Law No. 4925 and Istanbul Metropolitan Municipality Transportation Coordination Center Decisions

Definitions / Abbreviations

Campus : Sabancı University Tuzla Campus
City : Settlements within the boundaries of the Metropolitan Municipalities of Istanbul and Izmit provinces
University : Sabancı University
TS : Services Unit / Transportation Services Specialist

Regulations

1. Personnel Shuttles

1.1. Adhering to the relevant laws and regulations, services are provided to provide transportation to and from the workplace for the employees of the University, interns and subcontractors determined by the University. This service is provided in accordance with the Transportation Services Procedure (PSER-C930-0101).

1.2. Vehicle inspections are carried out in accordance with the Transportation Services Procedure (PSER-C930-0101) through the Vehicle Inspection Form (FSER-C930-0102).

2. Shift and Collection Services

2.1. University arrivals and departures for shift workers are arranged according to the hours and routes announced by TS.

3. University Shuttle Services

3.1. Adhering to the relevant laws and regulations, services are provided for the arrival and departure of all University members to and from the campus.

3.2. This service is provided in accordance with the Transportation Services Procedure (PSER-C930-0101).

4. Private Student Shuttles

4.1. In private student shuttles, services are provided to ensure the coordination between the student and the company.

5. On-Campus Ring Service

5.1. Services are provided to provide on-campus transportation at the time and route determined by UH.

6. Special Service Requests

6.1. Service is provided by the university units and faculties to provide special shuttle services for a specific purpose (artistic, sportive, academic, social, etc. activities). The said service is provided according to the Transportation Services Procedure (PSER-C930-0101).

7. Institution Vehicles

7.1. It is the responsibility of TS supply vehicles to be allocated to the personnel working at Sabancı University, who need to be allocated a vehicle due to their position or duty, and the allocation takes place as defined in the Vehicle Allocation and Use Procedure (PSER-S930-0102). In addition, the allocation of temporary vehicles to Sabancı University personnel due to their short-term duties is under the responsibility of TS and takes place as defined in the General Service Vehicle Procedure (PSER-S930-0103).
