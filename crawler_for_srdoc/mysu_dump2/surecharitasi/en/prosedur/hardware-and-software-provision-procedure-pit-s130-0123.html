Hardware and Software Provision Procedure (PIT-S130-0123)

Procedure No
PIT-S130-0123V14
Effective Date
13-01-2012
Update Date
13-03-2025
Related Unit / Owner
IT / Bilgi Teknolojileri Direktörü
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Related Instruction
Bilgi Teknolojileri İşlerini Yönetme Yönergesi (IIT-S130-01)
Aim
To provide up-to-date hardware and software capabilities that users may need due to their duties at the university, and to specify relevant methods and rules.
Scope
This procedure includes the provision of hardware and software capabilities necessary for SU employees and students.
Definitions / Abbreviations

SU: Sabancı University

IT: Information Technology

Suticket: Call Recording/Tracking Application

IKVKB: Internet and Corporate Data Usage Document

2. User Additional Requirements  

 

 

2.1. The provision of mobile phones and tablets is regulated according to information received from the HR Benefits Department.

Requests for &quot;laptop and desktop&quot; can only be procured if required by the job description and job description, with the approval of the requesting department manager and IT Director, and subject to budget availability.)

3. Departing User

 

 

3.1. A record is created in SUTicket based on the user information received from the Human Resources Department through the workflow application.

Human Resources

3.2.  Computers and mobile phones and tablets issued to users that are less than five years old, and mobile phones and tablets that are less than three years old, are returned in their entirety, and IT inventory records are updated. Equipment that has reached the fifth year of its book value for computers and the third year for phones and tablets is sold for a price determined according to its lifespan. University-licensed software on the sold equipment is removed, and hardware and software support is not provided.

If the equipment is partially or completely unusable due to negligence or violation of the usage rules specified on the delivery note, the user must pay the replacement cost according to the terms and conditions specified on the delivery note.
