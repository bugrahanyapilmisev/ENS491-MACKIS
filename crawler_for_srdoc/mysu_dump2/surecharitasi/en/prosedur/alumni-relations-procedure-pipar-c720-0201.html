Aim
The aim of this procedure is to guide the implementation of the rules regarding the preparation and organization of various activities and services to be provided in the University's relations with its alumni.
Scope
This procedure covers the communication of SU with its alumni, alumni activities, alumni perception research, the meeting of the President with alumni candidates and the implementation steps in the Coordination processes with SUMED (Sabancı University Alumni Association).

1.2 Alumni Information Update

 

 

1.2.1 A campaign is prepared for alumni to update their information on the Alumni Web.

Important: In order to participate in the campaign, alumni must enter the "Alumni Web Profile Area" on the Alumni Web with the password of their SU email address and update their job and contact information at least. The information updated by the alumni is automatically reflected in the Alumni CRM Administration database.

AO / Alumni

 Alumni Information Update Campaign / Beginning of January - for a period of 1 month

1.2.2 While updating the information of the alumni, the following headings are expected to be updated.

  • The name of the company where they work or the name of the university where they continue their education
  • Industry of the company
  • Department they work in
  • Working position
  • Date of start
  • Office phone
  • Mobile phone
  • Home phone
  • Business address
  • Preferred mailing address
  • Business email address
  • Personal email address
  • Preferred email address for communication

Alumni

Alumni Information Update Headings / Throughout the Year

1.2.3 If the institution / organization where the graduate is located is not included in the database, the graduate fills in the "Other" field.

Alumni

 Institution – Organization Entry / Throughout the Campaign

1.2.4 The name of the institution / organization added to the system by the alumni is also added to the database and an informative e-mail is sent to the related graduate.

AO

Adding Institution –Organization to Database / During the Campaign

1.2.5 Reminder e-mails are sent to all e-mail addresses of alumni who do not participate in the campaign. Reminders are also made on social media.

AO

 Reminder to Alumni / Biweekly During the Campaign

1.2.6 A reminder SMS is sent to alumni who do not participate in the campaign.

AO

 SMS / 2 Times During the Campaign

1.2.7 After the campaign, the list of all alumni whose information is not up to date since the beginning of the year is drawn through SU Reports and the Alumni Information Update Form (FIPAR-C72002- 01) is sent to these alumni by e-mail to update their information. The current information of the alumni is added to the email sent and alumni are asked to update this information.

AO / Alumni

 Information Update Form Email/ During 2 Weeks Following the Campaign

1.2.8 Updated information is added to the Alumni CRM Administration database.

AO

 Current Alumni Information / Mid-February

1.2.9 The remaining out-of-date alumni list (excluding the alumni of that year) is shared with the contracted call center company.

AO

 Out-of-date Alumni List (Excluding alumni of That Year) / Mid-February

1. Cell Phone

2. Business Phone

3. The switchboard phone of the company they work for

4. Home phone

5. Parent phone

The information received is recorded in the data pattern table prepared by the IT unit.

Contracted Call Center Firm

 Information Update Alumni Calls / For Three Weeks in March

AO

AO

1.3 Alumni Analysis and Sharing

 

 

1.3.1 The following indicators, which may change from year to year, are included in the analyzes prepared each year.

  • Number of alumni by Faculty and Graduation Degrees
  • Number of Foreign Graduates and Their Countries
  • What Are Our Alumni Doing? (Overall and by Faculty)
  • Number of alumni Abroad by Continent
  • Distribution of alumni with post-graduate studies and/or jobs by Country
  • Which Universities Have the Most SU Graduates for Postgraduate Studies? (Overall and by Faculty)
  • Sectors in which our Undergraduate and Graduate Alumni Work (Overall and by Faculty)
  • Which Organizations employ Our alumni Most? (Overall and by Faculty)
  • Sectors of Our Undergraduate and Graduate Alumni
  • Departments of Our Undergraduate and Graduate Alumni
  • Positions of Our Undergraduate and Graduate Alumni
  • Placement Rate of alumni in One Year following Graduation
  • Our New Undergraduate Alumni Who Couldn't Be Placed
  • Which Programs?
  • All Our Undergraduate Alumni Who Couldn't Be Placed
  • Which Programs?

AO

Alumni Analysis / Early June

1.3.2 Completed analyzes are shared with BT Members, SU Administrators, and faculty members and used in Promotion Days. Information is updated on the Alumni Web page.

AO

Alumni Analysis / End of June

  • Alumni who stated that they would like to volunteer in the satisfaction surveys of previous events,
  • Alumni who fill out the volunteer form on the Alumni Web

AO

  • Outdoor Activities and Evening Entertainment

AO

  • Event schedule rollup
