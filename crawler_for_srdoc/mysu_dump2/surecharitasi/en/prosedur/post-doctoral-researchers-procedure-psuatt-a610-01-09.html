Aim
This procedure aims to determine the functioning and implementation methods for assigning postdoctoral researchers working in Sabancı University faculties/centers to projects.
Scope
This procedure lays out the implementation and management steps of the assignment of postdoctoral researchers.
Definitions / Abbreviations

1. Postdoctoral Researchers with Scholarships in TÜBİTAK ARDEB Projects

 

1.1. In TÜBİTAK ARDEB projects, if a postdoctoral researcher will be assigned to the project during the contract phase, the PP will ask the PI during the project application whether the person is a foreign national.

1.2. The PI informs the PU when the information about the postdoctoral researcher who will work on the project is finalized. PU fills out the TÜBİTAK-ARDEB Postdoctoral Researcher Form under the heading of Employment and Position Request Form in SUFORM.

1.3. Through the form, HR, UIO and the faculty/center are informed about the future of the researcher. SUFORM must include information on the nationality of the researcher who will come to SU, housing request, academic title, and the start and end date of the project.

1.4. Since the start and end dates of the project are specified, UİO is responsible for the researcher's visa procedures, HR is responsible for the accommodation procedures through the 'Residence Application Form (FHR-S470-10-01)', and the relevant person at the faculty/center is responsible for the faculty/center procedures

1.5. Based on the approved form, the documents requested by TÜBİTAK are prepared by PU and the request is made within the framework of TÜBİTAK's application principles.

1.6. After the request is approved by TÜBİTAK, PU will inform HR in writing by e-mail.

1.7. A 'TÜBİTAK Research Projects Postdoctoral Researcher Agreement' is signed between the scholarship holder who will take part in the project and SU.

1.8. The difference between the fees of TÜBİTAK-supported postdoctoral researchers and the fees of faculty/center postdoctoral researchers can be covered by the relevant faculty/center.

2.       Postdoctoral Researchers Working in EU and Commercial R&D Projects

2.1    If the PI wants to appoint a postdoctoral researcher in their project, they submit this request through the Employment and Position Request Form in SUFORM. This form includes information such as whether the person is a foreign national, whether they have a work/residence permit, and if so, its duration. With this form, information is transmitted to HR after faculty/center approval.

2.2    In line with the information contained in the relevant form, the necessary procedures are initiated to obtain a work and residence permit, if necessary.

 2.3    For postdoctoral researchers who will be assigned to EU and Commercial R&D projects (after the foreign postdoctoral researcher receives a work and residence permit, written information is given to the HR Unit by e-mail.

2.4    HR carries out the employment procedures based on this form and then a contract is signed between the person and SU.

2.5    In EU and contracted R&D projects, there is no upper limit on the salary to be paid to a postdoctoral researcher, provided that the initial human resources budget of the project includes the salary foreseen for the period during which the postdoctoral researcher will work, all costs and fringe benefits to be covered by the project (without any cost to the university).

3.       Postdoctoral Researchers within the Scope of TÜBİTAK BİDEB

 

3.1    Information about researchers coming to SU through TÜBİTAK BİDEB projects is provided by PP to HR, UİO and the future faculty/center of the researcher through the TÜBİTAK BİDEB Support Form under the heading of Employment and Position Request Form in SUFORM. SUFORM includes information on the nationality of the researcher who will come to SU, housing request, academic title, and the start and end date of the project.

3.2    Since the start and end dates of the project are specified, UIO is responsible for the researcher's visa procedures, HR is responsible for the accommodation procedures through the "Residence Application Form (FHR-S470-10-01)", and the relevant person at the faculty/center is responsible for the faculty/center procedures.

4.       Assigning Postdoctoral Researchers in Faculties/Centers

 

4.1    The faculty/center forwards the information about the people who will work as Postdoctoral Researchers at the faculty/center to HR via the Academic Employment Request Form.

4.2    In line with the information in the relevant form, HR initiates the necessary procedures to obtain a work and residence permit, if necessary. See Work and Residence Permit Procedure (PHR-S450-07-01-V02)

 4.3    After obtaining a work and residence permit, recruitment procedures are carried out and the contract is prepared by HR.

 4.4    The salary of postdoctoral researchers on the university's payroll is determined by HR.

 

 5. Evaluation of the assignment of Postdoctoral Researchers to serve as principal investigators in the support programs (except 1004, 1007 and 1003) carried out by TÜBİTAK Research Support Programs Directorate (ARDEB)

 

5.1. Postdoctoral researchers who are currently involved in different projects at SU as scholarship holders or who are not on a permanent contract can apply as principal investigators in ARDEB programs with the approval of the relevant Faculty Dean or the relevant Unit/Center Director.

5.2. The requests of postdoctoral researchers who wish to apply through our university from a different institution other than SU are evaluated and decided by the relevant Faculty Dean/Unit/Center Director and the Vice President for Research.

5.3. For requests coming from outside SU, the completion of the TÜBİTAK ARDEB Project Application Request Form is provided by SUATT PP and the approval process is followed up through PP.
