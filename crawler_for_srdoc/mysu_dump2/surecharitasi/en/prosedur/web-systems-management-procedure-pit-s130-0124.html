Aim
To carry out the maintenance and operation activities of Sabancı University&#39;s websites and applications.
Scope
This procedure covers the maintenance and operation activities of websites and applications for which infrastructure services are provided by the Information Technologies (IT) Unit on behalf of the University.
Definitions / Abbreviations

SU: Sabancı University

IT: Information Technology

Unit: Sabancı University academic and administrative units

User: Sabancı University academic and administrative staff

IT Officer: Information Technologies Officer

PKI: Marketing and Institutional Communications

SUTicket: Call Recording/Tracking Application

D-IYS: Drupal Content Management System
