Related Instruction
Güvenlik Hizmetleri Yönergesi (ISER-C940-01)
Aim
It is to determine the principles, authorities and responsibilities in order to prevent undesirable situations with some simple measures and to prevent the emergence of such turmoil situations, when signs of demonstrations and riots are seen or at the very beginning concerning disputes between different student groups that arise for various reasons within the campus, or the provocations of illegal organizations, etc. illegal / unauthorized meetings, demonstrations, marches etc. in order to prevent situations of turmoil from getting out of control and becoming unstoppable.
Scope
It covers a process from the activities of eliminating the elements that will cause such turmoil to the post-event due diligence.
Definitions / Abbreviations

Sec. staff: Security staff
SCC: Security Control Center
Mngr: Manager

Implementation Steps:

Responsibility

Outputs / Timing

1. The security personnel, who hears of or encounters any riot, must perform the following duties, respectively, while arriving at the scene and after:

Security staff

Immediately upon receiving the warning

1.1. Informing: If possible, before reaching the scene, if not as soon as possible, security staff should try to learn the nature of the event. However, as mentioned before, they should not ask the conflicting parties without separating them.

Security staff

Immediately upon receiving the warning

1.2.  Notifying and Asking for Help: From the moment of arrival at the scene, the developments should be reported to the higher authorities and timely help should be requested. While reporting, the location of the event, the size of the crowd, its nature if known, and possible developments should be conveyed in clear terms. During the communication, due care should be taken to prevent the perceived uneasiness, possible dangerous developments and some private information from being heard by the crowd.

Security staff

Shift supervisor

Directorate of Security

From the moment of arrival at the scene

1.3.  Determining the Strategy: The Security Directorate should determine its strategy on how it should behave and what methods it should apply in order to end the riot, the nature of which it learned, and to disperse the mass.

Directorate of Security

Immediately upon receiving the warning

1.4. Removing the Elements of Provocation and Calming the Crowd: After contact with the crowd, the groups and persons causing the agitation are first distanced from each other and then from the crowd. The mass is sedated and dispersed by appropriate methods. An academic staff member assigned by the President’s Office assists the Security Manager to settle the incident.

Directorate of Security

Academic Staff in Charge

After contact with the mass

1.5. Observing the Crowd and Monitoring Developments: After the event is over and the crowd begins to disperse, security staff should leave the scene to show that the event is over. However, security staff should not distance completely and lose control. Caution should be exercised from a distance in case of regrouping and resumption of the event.

Directorate of Security

After the riot is over.

1.6. Personnel who have received First Aid training will assist people who are injured or sick due to the incident until the specialist medical team arrives at the scene.

Personnel with First Aid Training

Until the specialist medical team arrives at the scene

1.7. Shift Supervisor will send the appropriate security personnel to the scene and secure the surroundings.

Shift supervisor

 

Securing the environment

1.8. The personnel in the Security Control Center monitors and coordinates the effective and coordinated response of the security personnel to the incident and the execution of security services.

SCC Staff

 

2. Health Center’s Response and First Aid:

 

 

2.1 With the riot warning, the doctor in the Health Center will be ready to intervene with the necessary health equipment and personnel.

Health Center Manager

Immediately upon receiving the warning

3. Other Supports

 

 

3.1. Along with the riot warning, the Security Control Center will make a call for support to the police in the fastest way with the approval of the Security Manager.

SCC Staff

Call for immediate / police support when alerted.

3.2. The Academic Staff assigned by the President’s Office supports the Security Manager in pacifying the incident before it escalates.

President’s Office

Academic Staff in Charge

Calming the incident before it escalates

4. Training:

 

 

4. 1. Training of security personnel in response to riots will be monitored and controlled by the Security Directorate.

Directorate of Security

Riot response training

5. Reporting:

 

 

5. 1. According to the first observations and evaluations regarding the incident that occurred, a report containing the casualties, damages and losses of the incident will be prepared by the Security Directorate.

Directorate of Security

Incident Report

5.2. All personnel on duty at the time of the incident will report all developments to the Security Control Center. The Security Control Center will log all reported issues.

SCC Staff

Incident Report
