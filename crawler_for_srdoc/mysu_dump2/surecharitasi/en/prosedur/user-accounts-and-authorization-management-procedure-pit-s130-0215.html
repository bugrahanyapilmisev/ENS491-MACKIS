Aim
Managing user accounts and access to IT services
Scope
This procedure covers opening and closing user accounts and granting access to services in the IT service catalog.
Definitions / Abbreviations

HR: Human Resources

IT: Information Technologies

SUTicket: Call Recording/Tracking Application

SU-Net Account: A uniquely defined account used for accessing and authorizing IT services

8.1. Upon notification of withdrawal from Student Resources, the departing student will receive an informational message regarding account closure. The account will be closed on the closure date specified in the message.

8.2. Accounts of graduating students will not be closed. The email address and domain will be adjusted to firstname.lastname@alumni.sabanciuniv.edu. If the first name and last name have previously been assigned to another user, the user will be consulted regarding their preference for a second name. The old email address will be forwarded to the newly created address.

13. Password Rules

    

13.1. The password rules for the SU-Net account are as follows:

  • The password must be at least 8 characters.
  • The new password must be different from the previous password.
  • All characters must not be numbers.
  • It should not be the same as any password used in external environments such as social media. 

 

Users (student/staff)

 Password /

14. Email signatures and information messages:

    

14.1. Employee email signatures are added to the central email system based on HR information. They cannot be changed in a way that would harm the university's corporate image. Support for signature changes in the email client is not provided.

14.2. Informational messages (conferences, promotions, etc.) to be added to the email subfolder may be requested by the PKI or the Rectorate/General Secretariat. This information is applied to all email addresses by IT.
