Event Participation And Travel Support Procedure (PSUATT-A610-01-08)

Effective Date
31-03-2023

24-04-2025

Related Instruction
Araştırma Projeleri Süreçlerinin Yönetilmesi Yönergesi (ISUATT-A610-01)
Aim
The aim of this procedure is to determine the event participation and travel support usage rules for the development of International Research Projects provided by SUATT.
Scope
This procedure lays out the application, evaluation and finalization processes for event participation and travel support to be given to SU members for the development of research projects.
Definitions / Abbreviations

EU: European Union

BS: Applicant

Mİ: Financial Affairs

PP: Faculty of Engineering and Natural Sciences Project Partners, Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences, and Sabancı Business School Project Partners

SUATT: Research, Development and Technology Transfer Office Directorate

SU: Sabancı University

Implementation Steps

Responsibility

Outputs/Timing
1. Application Process

 

 

1.1. SU members request support from SUATT to participate in events (information day, project market, preliminary consortium meeting, idea gathering workshops and training on these subjects) in order to be involved in EU and other international projects.

1.2. The "Travel Support Application Form" filled out by the SU Member is forwarded to SUATT physically or by  e-mail.

1.3. The suitability of the submitted form is checked by SUATT and changes are requested in cases requiring revision.

1.4. The evaluation process is started for the application form determined to be suitable.

Applicant/SUATT

Travel Support Application Form for the Development of Research Projects

 

At least 1 month before the relevant project call closes

 
2. Evaluation and Acceptance Process

 

 

2.1.  Applications are evaluated by the SUATT Director and the relevant PP Manager within 1 week at the latest. The SUATT Director makes the final decision.

2.2.  Participation and Travel support confirmation letter is shared with the relevant SU member by e-mail on the same day.

SUATT Director/ PP Manager

Support Approval Letter

 
3. Usage of the Support

 

 

3.1. According to the principles specified in the Domestic/International Travel Expenses Procedure (PFA-S340-03V09), the "Travel Confirmation and Advance Form and Ticket Request Form (FFA-S34003-01)" are filled out by the SU member and submitted to SUATT.

3.2. Within the scope of the support, per diem and travel expenses are covered from the SUATT Exchange Program Budget.

3.3. Per diems and travel expenses to be covered from the relevant budget can have a duration of a maximum of 5 business days.

3.4.  In case of event/training participation expenses within the scope of support, the relevant SU member can declare the expense after the event or the expense can be covered by pre-event procurement method.

Applicant/SUATT/Mİ

Travel and ticket confirmation form

 

Event participation certificate/invoice
4. Procedures after Event and Travel

 

 

4.1. According to the principles specified in the Domestic/International Travel Expenses Procedure (PFA-S340-03V09), the "International Travel Expenses Declaration Form (FFA-S34003-02)" is filled out by the SU member and submitted to Mİ.

4.2. A copy of the travel closure documents is forwarded to SUATT by the relevant SU Member.

4.3. After the trip, the SU Member informs the SUATT Director and the relevant SUATT office to evaluate the event they attended.
