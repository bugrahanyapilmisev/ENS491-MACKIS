Graduation Audit and Diploma Issuance Procedure (PSR-C240-0101)

Procedure No
PSR-C240-0101-V015
Effective Date
25-06-2002
Update Date
21-12-2023
Related Unit / Owner
SR / Öğrenci Kaynakları Direktörü
Approved by
BT / Board of Trustees
Related Instruction
Aim
It is to explain the implementation steps to be followed for the determination of students who are eligible for graduation as a result of the graduation audit within the framework of the relevant instruction letters and the rules specified in the directive, the issuance of their diplomas and the graduation ceremony.
Scope
It includes students who are eligible to study at Sabancı University and graduate from undergraduate and graduate programs.

1. GRADUATION AUDIT AND CREATION OF CANDIDATE GRADUATE LISTS

 

 

1.1. The SR makes the "Graduation Announcement" on the internal web by completing the necessary system settings for prospective graduate students to apply for graduation via SIS.

1.2. The students keep track of the courses they have taken each semester and how many SU and/or ECTS credits and courses they have left for graduation via SIS. Students who can obtain the right to graduate if they are successful in the courses they have taken in any semester they are registered, submit their graduation requests to the SC through the form they fill out electronically on SIS within the announced periods.

Graduate Candidate Student

1.3. The SR determines the graduate candidates who are expected to graduate and transmits the information about the candidates and the results of the examination to the Faculties/Institutes by stating them in the Candidate Graduate Lists (FSR-C24001-21) prepared on the basis of faculty / level.

5. DETERMINATION OF FACULTY HIGH RANKED PRIZE WINNERS AMONG THE STUDENTS GRADUATED FROM UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS

 

 

5.1. Among the Fall and Spring semester graduates of the academic year to which the graduation ceremony belongs, those who have studied at SU for at least 6 and at most 9 semesters must be evaluated within the framework of GPA (Grades and Academic Status Evaluation Directive (ISR-C230-01) separately for each Faculty). ) sorted by height.

 

The student with the highest GPA in the rankings is determined as the graduate who will receive the first prize of the faculty he graduated from. If students enrolled in a double major diploma program at a faculty other than the faculty of their major graduate from both the major and double major diploma programs in the same period, it is ranked first both in the faculty where the major diploma program is affiliated and in the faculty where the double major diploma program is affiliated. These students are evaluated in both faculties with the GPA included in their transcripts.

 

The students who graduate from the double major program at the end of the additional period are ranked first in the faculty to which the double major diploma program is affiliated, with their GPA on their transcripts during the double major graduation period. If the student who graduates from the double major program in an academic year different from the academic year in which he graduated from the major program after the additional periods is entitled to the faculty top award as a result of the faculty top award rankings for the academic year in which he previously graduated from the major program, it is not included in the faculty first place award ranking (evaluation) for the academic year in which the student graduated from the double major program. 

 

The GPA calculations, which will be the basis for evaluation, are made within the framework of the Instruction for Determination of Faculty Top Ranks (FSR-C24001-37), taking into account the grades submitted up to 2 business days before the graduation ceremony and entered into the Student Information System.  

In case there is more than one candidate with equal GPA;

  • The one with the higher GPA in the last semester is entitled to the faculty first prize.
  • If the GPA of the last semester remains equal, the faculty with the higher GPA of the previous semester is entitled to the first place award.
  • If there is still a tie, the students in question will share the faculty first prize. 

SR

Determination of Faculty First Prize Winners / 2 business days before the Graduation Ceremony date 

5.2. Those who are awarded the faculty first prize are announced at the graduation ceremony. They will be given the "Faculty High Ranked Graduates - Sakıp Sabancı Award" in the week following the ceremony. The reward is given in the equivalent of 40 Republic Gold.  However, in case a student enrolled in a double major diploma program at a faculty other than the faculty of his/her major is entitled to the first prize in both faculties, he/she will only be given an award equivalent to 40 Republic Gold (2x40 = 80 Republic Gold equivalent award will not be given).

The award payment is made to the account number provided by the student, and the Republic Gold unit closing sales price announced 2 business days before the graduation ceremony is taken as basis in determining the value of the Republic Gold.

In case there is more than one Faculty high ranked student, the award will be shared equally.

President

" Faculty High Ranked Graduates - Sakıp Sabancı Award" / Within the week after the Graduation Ceremony

SR

As of the end of courses

6.2. The SR makes the announcement explaining the graduation procedures on the internal web.

SR

Liability Announcement /

As of the end of courses

6.3. The student is required to pay the diploma fee and fulfill all obligations towards the University within the framework of the University Withdrawal Procedure (PSR-C210-0202).

Student

Procedures Required to Obtain a Diploma / Individually for those who will receive a diploma

6.4. The obligation status of the graduates is checked by the relevant units, and the relevant unit gives approval via the web for the students who have completed their obligations. These units are: BM, MI, Dormitories, BT, Faculty/Institute where the student is registered, IPAR and SR Unit.

BM /

Mİ /

Dormitories / IT / Faculties / Institutes /

IPAR / SR

Liability Control / Individually before diploma delivery

6.5. The receipt showing that the diploma fee has been paid is checked by the SR. "Disconnection Form for Graduates (FSR-C24001-39)" is prepared for graduates who pay the diploma fee and complete their unit obligations and is kept in the student file. The students who complete the graduation procedures are also required to personally sign the Diploma Book in the SR in order to receive a diploma. For the students who request to receive their diploma by mail, a registered postal card stating that the mail has been delivered to them is required to be kept together with the diploma book instead of a signature.

8. DIPLOMA SUPPLEMENT

 

 

8.1. Upon request, a Diploma Supplement is issued to students whose graduation is finalized and the first copy is delivered free of charge.

SR

Preparation and Delivery of Diploma Supplement / On demand

8.2. A Diploma Supplement may be issued to the graduate student again upon request. In this case, the student pays the Diploma Supplement fee determined for the relevant year by the Board of Trustees upon the recommendation of the Presidential Board and the relevant bank receipt is submitted to the SR.

Student

Diploma Supplement Request / On demand

10. NEW DIPLOMA ISSUANCE AS A RESULT OF THE LOSS OF THE DIPLOMA

 

 

10.1. Once a diploma has been awarded, a second diploma can be awarded only once and only upon the application of the student stating that he has lost his diploma. If the diploma issued for the second time is lost, it is not possible to issue a diploma again. The student, who wanted to get a new diploma because he lost it, placed an advertisement in one of the newspapers published in Turkey and listed on the Press Advertisement Agency's website, saying "I have lost my diploma, which I received from Sabancı University in the year '...", it is invalid." can request a new diploma to be issued by applying to the SU with the receipt of the fee and a petition. 

Student

New Diploma Application / When necessary

10.2. The SR completes the necessary procedures for the new diploma printing (printing the diploma by the printing house, completing the signatures, etc.) and delivers the diploma to the graduate by stamping "SECOND ISSUE" on the diploma.

SR

Second Diploma / At the time of the first diploma issuance after the student's application
