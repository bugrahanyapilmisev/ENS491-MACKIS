Aim
The use and maintenance of containers designed for use in emergencies and disasters, and the determination of responsible parties.
Scope
This procedure covers the Emergency and Disaster Containers and their contents located on the Sabancı University Tuzla campus.
Definitions / Abbreviations

SU: Sabancı University

Disaster Container: A steel room (cabin) containing the necessary equipment, hand tools, medical supplies, tools, and equipment to conduct search, rescue, protection, and first aid operations at a minimum level in emergencies and disasters.

Security Unit: The unit that ensures security within the Sabancı University campus.

Sabancı University Disaster Advisor: The person/institution that provides services to Sabancı University to determine the course of action in disasters and emergencies.

Disaster Container Use

The use of disaster containers located on the SU Tuzla campus is the responsibility of the Security Unit. Disaster container doors are padlocked to prevent unauthorized use. The Security Unit holds the keys, and in the event of an emergency or need for use, the doors are unlocked with the knowledge of the security shift supervisor.

Security Unit

Disaster container

Opening the container door and using the materials and equipment inside are prohibited except in disasters and emergencies, as well as for periodic maintenance and inspections.

Security Unit

Disaster container

The opening of disaster containers and the use of equipment/materials are authorized by Technical Services and Security Unit personnel. The Security Supervisor or authorized representative will not permit use by personnel outside of these units.

Periodic Inspection and Maintenance of Disaster Containers and Their Contents

Inventory, inspection, and maintenance of disaster containers and their contents will be carried out by the Technical Services, Security, and Health units every six months, depending on the type of material. One Electrical and one Mechanical personnel are assigned to Technical Services.

Training of Personnel Regarding Disaster Containers

Technical Services personnel responsible for disaster containers will provide necessary training annually to Security and Technical Services personnel on the contents, purpose, and method of use of the materials inside the container.

This training is hands-on. It is given by the SU Emergency and Disaster Consultant and recorded with the OHS Training Participation Form (FPOP-S210-01-07).
